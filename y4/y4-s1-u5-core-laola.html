《乘风破浪的女孩》 The Girl Who Rode the Wind and Broke the Waves

1 对duì劳láo拉lā而ér言yán，海hǎi洋yáng就jiù像xiàng她tā的de家jiā。她tā的de父fù母mǔ都dōu是shì经jīng验yàn丰fēng富fù的de海hǎi员yuán。他tā们men经jīng历lì了le7年nián的de环huán球qiú航háng海hǎi旅lǚ程chéng。劳láo拉lā在zài这zhè期qī间jiān出chū世shì，因yīn此cǐ，她tā5岁suì之zhī前qián都dōu住zhù在zài海hǎi上shàng。
1For Laura, the ocean was like her home. Her parents were experienced sailors who undertook a seven-year voyage around the world. Laura was born during the trip — so until she was five years old, she lived at sea.
2 劳láo拉lā6岁suì那nà年nián，看kàn到dào爸bà爸bà亲qīn手shǒu建jiàn造zào了le一yì艘sōu船chuán。她tā当dāng时shí虽suī然rán只zhǐ是shì一yí个gè小xiǎo帮bāng手shǒu，但dàn这zhè件jiàn事shì让ràng她tā深shēn受shòu启qǐ发fā。她tā也yě在zài同tóng一yì年nián建jiàn造zào了le自zì己jǐ的de小xiǎo木mù筏fá。
2The year Laura was six, she watched her father build a boat with his own hands. She was only a small helper at the time, but the experience left a deep impression on her. That same year, she built her own little raft.
3 8岁suì那nà年nián，劳láo拉lā想xiǎng要yào购gòu买mǎi一yī艘sōu帆fān船chuán。为wèi了le赚zhuàn钱qián，她tā从cóng事shì不bù同tóng的de工gōng作zuò，例lì如rú派pài送sòng报bào纸zhǐ，在zài街jiē头tóu表biǎo演yǎn等děng。在zài存cún够gòu钱qián买mǎi船chuán之zhī前qián，她tā说shuō服fú了le爸bà爸bà的de朋péng友yǒu把bǎ一yī艘sōu旧jiù船chuán借jiè给gěi她tā，让ràng她tā练liàn习xí航háng海hǎi的de技jì术shù。11岁suì时shí，她tā独dú自zì驾jià驶shǐ这zhè艘sōu旧jiù船chuán在zài荷hé兰lán外wài海hǎi航háng行xíng了le7个gè星xīng期qī。
3At eight, Laura set her heart on buying a sailboat. To earn money, she took on all kinds of jobs — delivering newspapers, busking in the street, and more. Before she'd saved enough to buy a boat, she convinced one of her father's friends to lend her an old one so she could practise her sailing. At eleven, she alone sailed that old boat in the waters off the Dutch coast for seven weeks.
4 劳láo拉lā后hòu来lái存cún了le足zú够gòu的de钱qián，买mǎi了le一yī艘sōu帆fān船chuán，实shí现xiàn了le拥yōng有yǒu船chuán只zhī的de梦mèng想xiǎng。于yú是shì，她tā把bǎ时shí间jiān都dōu花huā在zài练liàn习xí航háng行xíng和hé维wéi修xiū船chuán只zhī上shàng。13岁suì时shí，她tā独dú自zì从cóng荷hé兰lán航háng行xíng至zhì英yīng国guó。当dāng时shí，她tā充chōng满mǎn自zì信xìn，制zhì定dìng了le环huán球qiú航háng行xíng计jì划huà。她tā觉jué得de自zì己jǐ不bù但dàn有yǒu船chuán和hé经jīng费fèi，而ér且qiě掌zhǎng握wò了le航háng海hǎi的de技jì术shù。她tā相xiāng信xìn这zhè个ge计jì划huà一yí定dìng能néng成chéng功gōng。
4Laura eventually saved enough to buy a sailboat — fulfilling her dream of owning a vessel. From then on, she poured her time into practising sailing and maintaining the boat. At thirteen, she sailed alone from the Netherlands to England. Brimming with confidence, she drew up a plan to sail around the world. She felt she now had the boat, the funds, and the seafaring skills. She believed her plan was bound to succeed.
5 没méi想xiǎng到dào，荷hé兰lán政zhèng府fǔ却què认rèn为wéi劳láo拉lā的de年nián纪jì太tài小xiǎo，不bù准zhǔn她tā独dú自zì出chū航háng。劳láo拉lā不bù放fàng弃qì，她tā相xiāng信xìn只zhǐ要yào有yǒu决jué心xīn，就jiù能néng实shí现xiàn梦mèng想xiǎng。所suǒ谓wèi“有yǒu志zhì者zhě，事shì竟jìng成chéng”，劳láo拉lā极jí力lì争zhēng取qǔ，终zhōng于yú在zài10个gè月yuè后hòu获huò得dé批pī准zhǔn，可kě以yǐ独dú自zì出chū航háng。
5But, to her surprise, the Dutch government decided she was too young, and refused to let her sail solo. Laura didn't give up. She believed that with determination, dreams could be realised. As the saying goes, "Where there's a will, there's a way." Laura fought hard for her right — and after ten months, she was finally granted permission to set sail alone.
6 14岁suì的de劳láo拉lā乘chéng船chuán离lí开kāi荷hé兰lán，展zhǎn开kāi了le一yí个gè人rén的de环huán球qiú航háng行xíng。17个gè月yuè后hòu，她tā结jié束shù航háng行xíng。年nián仅jǐn16岁suì的de她tā成chéng为wéi完wán成chéng这zhè项xiàng壮zhuàng举jǔ最zuì年nián轻qīng的de航háng海hǎi家jiā！
6At fourteen, Laura set sail from the Netherlands and began her solo voyage around the world. Seventeen months later, she completed her journey. At just sixteen, she became the youngest sailor ever to achieve this feat!
7 劳láo拉lā勇yǒng于yú追zhuī梦mèng、不bù轻qīng言yán放fàng弃qì。她tā的de勇yǒng气qì和hé决jué心xīn，让ràng她tā能néng在zài海hǎi洋yáng与yǔ人rén生shēng中zhōng乘chéng风fēng破pò浪làng，奋fèn勇yǒng前qián进jìn。
7Laura had the courage to chase her dreams, and refused to give up easily. Her courage and resolve let her ride the wind and break the waves — both on the ocean and through life — and press on with everything she had.
