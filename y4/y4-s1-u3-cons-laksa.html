1 刚gāng到dào加jiā拿ná大dà时shí，我wǒ常cháng常cháng梦mèng到dào自zì己jǐ跟gēn朋péng友yǒu或huò家jiā人rén坐zuò在zài新xīn加jiā坡pō的de小xiǎo贩fàn中zhōng心xīn。桌zhuō上shàng摆bǎi满mǎn了le美měi食shí，香xiāng气qì扑pū鼻bí，其qí中zhōng少shào不bù了liǎo我wǒ最zuì爱ài的de叻lè沙shā。正zhèng当dāng我wǒ准zhǔn备bèi大dà吃chī一yī顿dùn时shí，梦mèng醒xǐng了le。那nà种zhǒng失shī落luò感gǎn可kě想xiǎng而ér知zhī。
1When I first arrived in Canada, I often dreamed of sitting in a Singapore hawker centre with friends or family. The table was piled with delicious food, the fragrance hitting me — and there, of course, was my favourite laksa. Just as I was about to dig in — I'd wake up. You can imagine that sinking feeling.
2 在zài加jiā拿ná大dà求qiú学xué的de日rì子zi，我wǒ和hé同tóng学xué一yī有yǒu机jī会huì就jiù去qù寻xún找zhǎo新xīn加jiā坡pō美měi食shí，但dàn大dà部bù分fèn的de新xīn加jiā坡pō美měi食shí都dōu有yǒu名míng无wú实shí，更gèng别bié说shuō是shì好hǎo吃chī的de叻lè沙shā了le。唯wéi一yī让ràng我wǒ们men回huí头tóu的de，是shì一yī家jiā名míng为wèi“狮shī城chéng”的de餐cān馆guǎn。这zhè里lǐ的de叻lè沙shā味wèi道dào还hái不bú错cuò，就jiù是shì太tài贵guì了le。有yǒu什shén么me办bàn法fǎ呢ne？毕bì竟jìng这zhè里lǐ是shì加jiā拿ná大dà。相xiāng比bǐ之zhī下xià，在zài新xīn加jiā坡pō的de生shēng活huó就jiù方fāng便biàn多duō了le。小xiǎo贩fàn中zhōng心xīn随suí处chù可kě见jiàn，不bù但dàn食shí物wù种zhǒng类lèi多duō，而ér且qiě价jià格gé也yě大dà众zhòng化huà。
2During my student days in Canada, whenever my classmates and I had the chance, we'd hunt down Singapore food. But most of it was Singaporean in name only — let alone any decent laksa. The only place we'd go back to was a restaurant called "Lion City." Their laksa wasn't bad, but it was just too expensive. Then again, what could we do? This was Canada, after all. By comparison, life in Singapore was so much easier. Hawker centres everywhere — not just a wide range of food, but reasonable prices for everyone.
3 想xiǎng着zhe念niàn着zhe家jiā乡xiāng的de叻lè沙shā，我wǒ终zhōng于yú盼pàn来lái了le假jià期qī。在zài回huí国guó的de班bān机jī上shàng，吃chī着zhe飞fēi机jī餐cān时shí，我wǒ的de舌shé尖jiān仿fǎng佛fú已yǐ经jīng尝cháng到dào熟shú悉xī的de本běn地dì美měi食shí。一yī回huí到dào新xīn加jiā坡pō，我wǒ就jiù迫pò不bù及jí待dài地dì拉lā着zhe妈mā妈mā到dào家jiā附fù近jìn的de小xiǎo贩fàn中zhōng心xīn。我wǒ直zhí接jiē叫jiào了le叻lè沙shā，还hái叫jiào了le福fú建jiàn炒chǎo虾xiā面miàn、沙shā爹diē、印yìn度dù煎jiān饼bǐng等děng美měi食shí。不bù一yí会huì儿r，我wǒ就jiù把bǎ食shí物wù吃chī了le个gè精jīng光guāng。妈mā妈mā笑xiào我wǒ是shì个gè“贪tān吃chī鬼guǐ”，可kě她tā哪nǎ里lǐ知zhī道dào，我wǒ“贪tān吃chī”是shì情qíng有yǒu可kě原yuán的de。这zhè些xiē看kàn似shì普pǔ通tōng的de食shí物wù，都dōu曾céng是shì我wǒ日rì思sī夜yè想xiǎng的de美měi食shí啊a！
3Thinking and longing for the laksa back home, I finally made it through to the holidays. On the flight home, eating the airline meal, it was as if my tongue could already taste those familiar local dishes. The moment I got back to Singapore, I couldn't wait — I dragged Mum off to the hawker centre near our home. I ordered laksa straight away, then Hokkien fried prawn noodles, satay, roti prata, and more. In no time, I'd polished off everything. Mum teased me, calling me a "greedy guts" — but how could she know that my "greed" was perfectly understandable? These dishes that looked so ordinary had been food I'd dreamed about, day and night.
4 留liú学xué几jǐ年nián，吃chī，是shì我wǒ们men常cháng说shuō常cháng新xīn的de话huà题tí。可kě是shì，对duì于yú家jiā乡xiāng的de情qíng感gǎn又yòu何hé止zhǐ是shì对duì这zhè些xiē美měi食shí的de思sī念niàn？在zài国guó外wài的de经jīng历lì让ràng我wǒ深shēn深shēn体tǐ会huì到dào，除chú了le家jiā乡xiāng的de美měi食shí，家jiā乡xiāng的de朋péng友yǒu、亲qīn人rén、景jǐng物wù等děng也yě是shì值zhí得de我wǒ们men好hǎo好hǎo珍zhēn惜xī的de。狮shī城chéng的de一yí切qiè，始shǐ终zhōng让ràng我wǒ念niàn念niàn不bù忘wàng。
4Over my years studying abroad, food was a topic that came up again and again. But how could the longing for home be limited to a longing for these dishes alone? My experience overseas gave me a deep understanding that besides the food back home, the friends, the family, the sights — all of these are worth treasuring. Every part of the Lion City stays with me always.
