1 我wǒ的de姑gū姑gū是shì一yī名míng护hù士shì。去qù年nián，奶nǎi奶nǎi生shēng病bìng住zhù院yuàn。在zài姑gū姑gū细xì心xīn照zhào顾gù下xià，奶nǎi奶nǎi渐jiàn渐jiàn康kāng复fù。
1My aunt is a nurse. Last year, Grandma fell ill and was hospitalised. Under my aunt's attentive care, Grandma gradually recovered.
2 姑gū姑gū在zài国guó家jiā传chuán染rǎn病bìng中zhōng心xīn工gōng作zuò。疫yì情qíng爆bào发fā时shí，她tā的de工gōng作zuò忙máng碌lù起qǐ来lái，常cháng常cháng加jiā班bān到dào深shēn夜yè，回huí到dào家jiā时shí已yǐ是shì精jīng疲pí力lì尽jìn。
2My aunt works at the National Centre for Infectious Diseases. When the pandemic broke out, her work grew busy — she often had to work overtime into the night, and was utterly drained by the time she got home.
3 虽suī然rán辛xīn苦kǔ，但dàn姑gū姑gū从cóng不bù抱bào怨yuàn。她tā说shuō，能néng帮bāng助zhù病bìng人rén恢huī复fù健jiàn康kāng，就jiù是shì最zuì幸xìng福fú的de事shì。
3Despite how hard it was, my aunt never complained. She says that being able to help patients get back to health is the happiest thing.
