《和外公吃早餐》 Breakfast with Grandpa

1 星xīng期qī天tiān早zǎo上shàng，外wài公gōng带dài我wǒ到dào小xiǎo贩fàn中zhōng心xīn吃chī早zǎo餐cān，那nà里lǐ热rè闹nào极jí了le。这zhè里lǐ有yǒu各gè种zhǒng各gè样yàng的de美měi食shí，从cóng马mǎ来lái族zú、印yìn度dù族zú到dào华huá族zú的de食shí物wù都dōu有yǒu。我wǒ们men可kě以yǐ一yì边biān吃chī，一yì边biān体tǐ验yàn多duō元yuán文wén化huà的de魅mèi力lì。
1On Sunday morning, my grandfather took me to the hawker centre for breakfast — the place was bustling with life. Here, there's all kinds of food — Malay, Indian, Chinese, all of it. We can eat and experience the charm of multicultural life at the same time.
2 外wài公gōng带dài我wǒ去qù他tā最zuì爱ài的de饮yǐn料liào摊tān位wèi，老lǎo板bǎn和hé他tā打dǎ招zhāo呼hū，看kàn来lái他tā们men已yǐ经jīng认rèn识shí很hěn多duō年nián了le。外wài公gōng点diǎn了le两liǎng份fèn套tào餐cān。
2Grandpa took me to his favourite drinks stall — the owner greeted him, looking like they'd known each other for many years. Grandpa ordered two sets.
3 套tào餐cān里lǐ有yǒu一yī杯bēi热rè腾téng腾téng的de咖kā啡fēi、两liǎng片piàn香xiāng喷pēn喷pēn的de咖kā椰yē牛niú油yóu烤kǎo面miàn包bāo，还hái有yǒu两liǎng个gè半bàn生shēng熟shú蛋dàn。外wài公gōng教jiào我wǒ把bǎ蛋dàn打dǎ开kāi，加jiā点diǎn酱jiàng油yóu和hé胡hú椒jiāo粉fěn，再zài用yòng面miàn包bāo蘸zhàn着zhe吃chī，味wèi道dào真zhēn是shì好hǎo极jí了le。这zhè就jiù是shì新xīn加jiā坡pō传chuán统tǒng的de早zǎo餐cān！
3Each set comes with a steaming hot kopi, two slices of fragrant kaya-butter toast, and two soft-boiled eggs. Grandpa taught me to crack the eggs open, add a splash of soy sauce and a sprinkle of pepper, then dip the toast in — the flavour was just wonderful. That's a traditional Singapore breakfast!
