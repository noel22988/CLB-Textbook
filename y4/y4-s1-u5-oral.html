1 “各gè位wèi观guān众zhòng，欢huān迎yíng收shōu看kàn狮shī城chéng论lùn坛tán。今jīn天tiān我wǒ们men讨tǎo论lùn的de话huà题tí是shì：新xīn加jiā坡pō人rén敢gǎn不bù敢gǎn冒mào险xiǎn？”主zhǔ持chí人rén林lín一yī明míng笑xiào着zhe说shuō。
1“Welcome, everyone, to the Lion City Forum. Today's topic for discussion is: do Singaporeans dare to take risks?” the moderator Lin Yiming said with a smile.
2 今jīn天tiān的de嘉jiā宾bīn有yǒu三sān位wèi：大dà学xué生shēng王wáng明míng丽lì、退tuì休xiū人rén士shì林lín美měi乐lè和hé企qǐ业yè家jiā黄huáng华huá强qiáng。
2Today's guest speakers are three: the university student Wang Mingli, the retiree Lin Meile, and the entrepreneur Huang Huaqiang.
3 王wáng明míng丽lì先xiān开kāi口kǒu：“沈shěn望wàng傅fù是shì新xīn加jiā坡pō科kē技jì领lǐng域yù富fù有yǒu传chuán奇qí色sè彩cǎi的de人rén物wù。他tā敢gǎn于yú冒mào险xiǎn，才cái能néng取qǔ得dé那nà么me大dà的de成chéng就jiù。”林lín美měi乐lè却què说shuō：“新xīn加jiā坡pō的de小xiǎo孩hái是shì否fǒu受shòu到dào过guò度dù的de保bǎo护hù？我wǒ们men应yīng该gāi让ràng他tā们men勇yǒng敢gǎn一yì点diǎn儿ér。”
3Wang Mingli spoke first: “Sim Wong Hoo is a legendary figure in Singapore's tech industry. It's exactly because he dared to take risks that he achieved so much.” Lin Meile, on the other hand, said: “Are Singapore's children too over-protected? We should let them be braver.”
4 黄huáng华huá强qiǎng笑xiào着zhe补bǔ充chōng：“其qí实shí，新xīn加jiā坡pō不bù缺quē少shǎo有yǒu创chuàng意yì又yòu愿yuàn意yì冒mào险xiǎn的de人rén。”大dà家jiā听tīng了le，都dōu不bù禁jīn点diǎn头tóu。
4Huang Huaqiang added with a smile: “In fact, Singapore doesn't lack creative people willing to take risks.” Hearing this, everyone nodded in agreement.
