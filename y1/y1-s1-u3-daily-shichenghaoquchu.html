乌wū节jié路lù
简jiǎn介jiè乌wū节jié路lù是shì著名zhù的míng旅游de购lǚ物yóu街gòu。乌wù节jiē路wū的jié名lù字de来míng自zì“Orchard”，意lái思zì是“Orchard”，果yì园si，因shì为guǒ以yuán前yīn这wèi条yǐ道qián路zhè的tiáo两dào旁lù都de是liǎng果páng园dōu。
Orchard Road
About. Orchard Road is a famous shopping street for tourists. Its name comes from the English word "Orchard" — meaning a fruit garden — because the road used to be lined with fruit orchards on both sides.
樟zhāng宜yí机jī场chǎng
简jiǎn介jiè樟zhāng宜yí机jī场chǎng世shì界jiè闻wén名míng，它tā也yě是shì人rén们men休闲xiū的xián好去de处hǎo。这qù里chù有zhè新lǐ奇yǒu的xīn游qí乐de设yóu施lè，还shè有shī来hái自yǒu世lái界zì各shì地jiè的gè美dì食de。
Changi Airport
About. Changi Airport is world-famous, and it's also a great place to relax. There are novel attractions, plus food from all around the world.
牛niú车chē水shuǐ
简jiǎn介jiè牛niú车chē水shuǐ是shì游yóu客kè们men必bì到dào的de地dì方fāng，这zhè里lǐ有yǒu不bù少shǎo售卖shòu特mài色礼tè品sè及lǐ美pǐn食jí的měi商shí店de。当shāng年diàn人dāng们nián用rén牛men车yòng载niú水chē来这zài里，牛shuǐ车lái水zhè因lǐ此niú得chē名shuǐ。
Chinatown
About. Chinatown is a must-visit for tourists, with plenty of shops selling unique gifts and food. In the old days, people used to bring water here in ox-drawn carts — and that's how the place got its Chinese name 牛车水 ("ox cart water").
白bái沙shā浮fú广guǎng场chǎng
简jiǎn介jiè白bái沙shā浮fú广guǎng场chǎng是shì年nián轻qīng人rén喜xǐ欢huān聚集jù的jí地方de。广dì场fāng内guǎng装chǎng有nèi玻zhuāng璃yǒu屋bō顶lí和wū冷dǐng气hé，在lěng阳qì光zài充yáng足guāng又chōng十zú分yòu凉shí爽fēn的liáng空shuǎng间de逛kōng街jiān、购guàng物jiē，是gòu一wù种shì享yī受zhǒng。
Bugis Junction
About. Bugis Junction is a popular hangout for young people. The complex is fitted with a glass roof and air-conditioning — strolling and shopping in this sunlit yet cool space is genuinely a treat.
