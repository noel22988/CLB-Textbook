《国家美术馆的“秘密”》 The "Secrets" of the National Gallery

语速 Speed 0.9× (set before play)
字体 Size

生词 Vocabulary · 2 words

幅fú
measure word for paintings or pictures
村cūn落luò
village; small settlement
1 新xīn加jiā坡pō国guó家jiā美měi术shù馆guǎn占zhàn地dì6万wàn4000平píng方fāng米mǐ，大dà约yuē有yǒu9个gè足zú球qiú场chǎng那nà么me大dà。
1The National Gallery Singapore covers 64,000 square metres — about the size of nine football fields.
2 你nǐ知zhī道dào新xīn加jiā坡pō国guó家jiā美měi术shù馆guǎn藏cáng着zhe许xǔ多duō有yǒu趣qù的de“秘mì密mì”吗ma？
2Did you know the National Gallery Singapore is hiding plenty of fascinating "secrets"?
3 国guó家jiā美měi术shù馆guǎn是shì由yóu前qián最zuì高gāo法fǎ院yuàn和hé政zhèng府fǔ大dà厦shà两liǎng座zuò大dà楼lóu合hé二èr为wéi一yī，改gǎi建jiàn而ér成chéng的de。国guó家jiā美měi术shù馆guǎn里lǐ有yǒu个gè地dì下xià通tōng道dào。那nà时shí，犯fàn人rén不bú是shì从cóng大dà门mén进jìn来lái，而ér是shì从cóng这zhè个ge特tè别bié通tōng道dào直zhí接jiē进jìn入rù法fǎ庭tíng。
3The National Gallery was created by combining and converting two buildings — the former Supreme Court and City Hall. There's an underground passage inside. Back in the day, prisoners didn't come in through the front door — they were brought directly into the courtroom through this special passage.
4 国guó家jiā美měi术shù馆guǎn里lǐ有yǒu个gè悬xuán空kōng桥qiáo，桥qiáo两liǎng头tóu的de大dà门mén都dōu是shì从cóng原yuán本běn的de窗chuāng户hù改gǎi造zào而ér来lái的de。
4There's a suspended walkway inside the gallery, and the doors at both ends of it were converted from what used to be windows.
5 国guó家jiā美měi术shù馆guǎn的de圆yuán顶dǐng原yuán本běn不bú是shì铜tóng绿lǜ色sè，经jīng过guò多duō年nián的de氧yǎng化huà才cái变biàn成chéng我wǒ们men现xiàn在zài看kàn到dào的de颜yán色sè。
5The dome of the National Gallery wasn't originally that copper-green colour — it took many years of oxidation to become the colour we see today.
6 国guó家jiā美měi术shù馆guǎn里lǐ有yǒu一yī幅fú画huà《晒shài咸xián鱼yú》被bèi称chēng作zuò“钱qián包bāo里lǐ的de名míng作zuò”，因yīn为wèi它tā印yìn在zài50新xīn元yuán的de背bèi面miàn。这zhè幅fú画huà表biǎo现xiàn了le过guò去qù东dōng南nán亚yà沿yán海hǎi村cūn落luò中zhōng常cháng见jiàn的de生shēng活huó场chǎng景jǐng。
6Inside the gallery is a painting called Drying Salted Fish (《晒咸鱼》), nicknamed "the masterpiece in your wallet" — because it's printed on the back of the S$50 note. The painting captures a scene from daily life that was once common in coastal villages across Southeast Asia.
7 国guó家jiā美měi术shù馆guǎn的de“秘mì密mì”还hái有yǒu很hěn多duō，去qù走zǒu一yī走zǒu，看kàn一yī看kàn吧ba，说shuō不bù定dìng你nǐ也yě会huì有yǒu新xīn发fā现xiàn。
7The National Gallery has many more secrets to discover. Go take a walk and have a look — you might just find some of your own.
