《难忘的建筑之旅》 An Unforgettable Architectural Journey

to deal with; to have dealings with (something/someone)
1 老lǎo师shī让ràng我wǒ们men设shè计jì一yí个gè建jiàn筑zhù模mó型xíng。为wèi了le帮bāng我wǒ找zhǎo灵líng感gǎn，爸bà爸bà带dài我wǒ去qù看kàn看kàn建jiàn筑zhù大dà师shī贝bèi聿yù铭míng设shè计jì的de几jǐ座zuò建jiàn筑zhù。
1Our teacher asked us to design a model building. To help me find inspiration, Dad took me to see a few buildings designed by the master architect I. M. Pei.
2 我wǒ们men来lái到dào中zhōng央yāng商shāng业yè区qū，沿yán着zhe新xīn加jiā坡pō河hé漫màn步bù。爸bà爸bà指zhǐ着zhe不bù远yuǎn处chù的de华huá侨qiáo银yín行háng大dà楼lóu对duì我wǒ说shuō：“那nà座zuò大dà楼lóu的de外wài观guān很hěn像xiàng‘贝bèi’的de繁fán体tǐ字zì‘貝bèi’。古gǔ人rén把bǎ贝bèi壳ké当dàng作zuò钱qián来lái用yòng，而ér银yín行háng不bù就jiù是shì与yǔ钱qián打dǎ交jiāo道dào吗ma？这zhè个ge设shè计jì非fēi常cháng巧qiǎo妙miào！”
2We went to the Central Business District and strolled along the Singapore River. Dad pointed at the OCBC Centre in the distance and said, "The shape of that tower looks just like the traditional form of the character 贝 (bèi) — written 貝. In ancient times, people used cowrie shells as money, and a bank deals with money — doesn't it? It's a clever design!"
3 我wǒ们men沿yán着zhe桥qiáo北běi路lù走zǒu，不bù久jiǔ就jiù看kàn到dào远yuǎn处chù的de来lái福fú士shì城chéng大dà楼lóu。这zhè座zuò楼lóu从cóng一yí面miàn看kàn是shì个gè圆yuán柱zhù体tǐ，从cóng另lìng一yí面miàn看kàn又yòu是shì个gè长cháng方fāng体tǐ。
3We walked along North Bridge Road, and soon saw Raffles City rising in the distance. From one side, the building looks like a cylinder; from another, it looks like a rectangular block.
4 离lí开kāi来lái福fú士shì城chéng，我wǒ们men又yòu顺shùn着zhe美měi芝zhī路lù来lái到dào新xīn门mén广guǎng场chǎng。这zhè里lǐ有yǒu两liǎng座zuò大dà楼lóu，好hǎo像xiàng两liǎng扇shàn打dǎ开kāi的de大dà门mén。大dà楼lóu形xíng状zhuàng特tè别bié，看kàn上shàng去qù就jiù像xiàng刀dāo片piàn一yí样yàng。
4Leaving Raffles City, we walked along Beach Road to The Gateway. Here there are two towers, like two open gates. Their shape is very distinctive — they look just like blades.
5 回huí到dào家jiā后hòu，我wǒ上shàng网wǎng查chá找zhǎo资zī料liào，对duì贝bèi聿yù铭míng先xiān生shēng有yǒu了le更gèng多duō的de了liǎo解jiě，对duì建jiàn筑zhù设shè计jì产chǎn生shēng了le浓nóng厚hòu的de兴xìng趣qù。
5After getting home, I went online to look up more information. I learned more about I. M. Pei — and I developed a deep interest in architectural design.
