《迟到》 Late

语速 Speed 0.9× (set before play)
字体 Size

生词 Vocabulary · 6 words

早zǎo晨chén
early morning
赖lài床chuáng
to laze in bed; to refuse to get up
哀āi求qiú
to plead; to beg sorrowfully
瞪dèng
to glare at; to stare (sternly)
仍réng然rán
still; nevertheless
罚fá
to punish (e.g. to make stand outside class)
1 以yǐ前qián，我wǒ有yǒu早zǎo晨chén喜xǐ欢huān赖lài床chuáng的de坏huài习xí惯guàn。
1I used to have a bad habit of lazing in bed every morning.
2 有yǒu一yì天tiān，下xià大dà雨yǔ，我wǒ醒xǐng来lái就jiù已yǐ经jīng不bù早zǎo了le。一yī想xiǎng到dào上shàng学xué要yào迟chí到dào，而ér且qiě还hái要yào冒mào着zhe雨yǔ走zǒu去qù学xué校xiào，我wǒ就jiù赖lài在zài床chuáng上shàng不bù起qǐ来lái了le。
2One day, it was pouring with rain, and by the time I woke up it was already late. The thought of being late for school — and having to walk there in the rain — was enough to make me stay curled up in bed.
3 妈mā妈mā进jìn来lái，我wǒ低dī声shēng哀āi求qiú她tā：
3Mum came in, and I pleaded with her quietly:
4 “妈mā，今jīn天tiān晚wǎn了le，我wǒ就jiù不bù去qù上shàng学xué了le吧ba？”
4"Mum, I'm already late today — can I just skip school?"
5 这zhè时shí，爸bà爸bà进jìn来lái了le。他tā站zhàn在zài床chuáng前qián，瞪dèng着zhe我wǒ：
5Just then, Dad came in. He stood by the bed and glared at me:
6 “怎zěn么me还hái不bù起qǐ来lái？起qǐ来lái！快kuài起qǐ来lái！”
6"Why aren't you up yet? Get up! Up, now!"
7 “晚wǎn了le！爸bà！”
7"It's late, Dad!"
8 “晚wǎn了le也yě得dé去qù！怎zěn么me可kě以yǐ逃táo学xué？起qǐ！”
8"Late or not, you're going! How can you skip school? Up!"
9 我wǒ有yǒu些xiē害hài怕pà，但dàn仍réng然rán不bù愿yuàn起qǐ来lái。爸bà爸bà气qì极jí了le，把bǎ我wǒ拖tuō起qǐ来lái，打dǎ了le我wǒ一yí下xià。好hǎo疼téng啊a！
9I was a little scared, but still wouldn't get up. Dad lost his temper, dragged me up, and gave me a smack. It really hurt!
10 最zuì后hòu，我wǒ被bèi女nǚ佣yōng抱bào上shàng人rén力lì车chē，第dì一yī次cì花huā钱qián坐zuò车chē上shàng学xué。
10In the end, our maid carried me onto a rickshaw, and for the first time I rode to school with money paid for the fare.
11 虽suī然rán迟chí到dào了le，但dàn因yīn为wèi下xià雨yǔ，我wǒ没méi有yǒu被bèi罚fá站zhàn。上shàng课kè前qián，老lǎo师shī叫jiào我wǒ们men先xiān闭bì上shàng眼yǎn，想xiǎng一yī想xiǎng做zuò过guò的de事shì情qíng。就jiù在zài这zhè时shí，老lǎo师shī告gào诉sù我wǒ，爸bà爸bà来lái了le，就jiù在zài教jiào室shì外wài面miàn。
11Even though I was late, I wasn't made to stand outside as punishment because of the rain. Before class began, the teacher asked us to close our eyes and think back over the things we'd done. Just then, the teacher told me that Dad had come — he was right outside the classroom.
12 我wǒ来lái到dào爸bà爸bà面miàn前qián。他tā没méi说shuō什shén么me，拿ná出chū我wǒ的de外wài套tào，看kàn着zhe我wǒ穿chuān上shàng，又yòu给gěi了le我wǒ两liǎng块kuài钱qián。
12I went out to face Dad. He said nothing — just took out my coat, watched as I put it on, and handed me two dollars.
13 从cóng那nà以yǐ后hòu，我wǒ再zài也yě没méi迟chí到dào过guò。每měi天tiān早zǎo晨chén，我wǒ都dōu是shì等děng待dài着zhe校xiào工gōng开kāi校xiào门mén的de学xué生shēng之zhī一yī。
13After that day, I was never late again. Every morning, I was one of the students waiting for the school caretaker to open the gates.
