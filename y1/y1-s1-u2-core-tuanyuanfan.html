《团圆饭》 The Reunion Dinner

1 农nóng历lì新xīn年nián悄qiāo悄qiāo地de走zǒu近jìn了le。妈mā妈mā开kāi始shǐ忙máng起qǐ来lái，装zhuāng饰shì家jiā里lǐ，买mǎi年nián货huò，为wèi团tuán圆yuán饭fàn做zuò准zhǔn备bèi。
1Lunar New Year was creeping closer. Mum started getting busy — decorating the house, buying festive goods, getting ready for the reunion dinner.
2 去qù年nián年nián初chū，姐jiě姐jiě结jié婚hūn，嫁jià到dào英yīng国guó去qù了le。当dāng兵bīng的dī哥gē哥gē现xiàn在zài还hái在zài国guó外wài受shòu训xùn。他tā们men都dōu不bù能néng回huí来lái吃chī团tuán圆yuán饭fàn。
2At the start of last year, my elder sister got married and moved to England. My elder brother, who's doing his National Service, is currently overseas for training. Neither of them can come home for the reunion dinner.
3 妈mā妈mā有yǒu些xiē不bù开kāi心xīn，买mǎi年nián货huò时shí黑hēi着zhe脸liǎn，处chǔ理lǐ鱼yú时shí皱zhòu着zhe眉méi。看kàn着zhe妈mā妈mā难nán过guò的de样yàng子zi，我wǒ想xiǎng到dào了le一yí个gè办bàn法fǎ，我wǒ拿ná起qǐ电diàn话huà，打dǎ给gěi了le姐jiě姐jiě和hé哥gē哥gē……
3Mum was a little down — buying CNY goods with a sullen face, frowning as she prepared the fish. Seeing her looking so sad, I thought of an idea. I picked up the phone and called my sister and brother…
4 转zhuǎn眼yǎn除chú夕xī到dào了le。吃chī过guò午wǔ饭fàn，平píng时shí不bù做zuò家jiā务wù的de爸bà爸bà也yě开kāi始shǐ洗xǐ菜cài、切qiè菜cài，准zhǔn备bèi晚wǎn上shàng的de火huǒ锅guō料liào。妈mā妈mā准zhǔn备bèi晚wǎn上shàng蒸zhēng鱼yú的de一yì些xiē材cái料liào。
4Before we knew it, New Year's Eve had arrived. After lunch, even Dad — who usually doesn't do any housework — started washing and chopping vegetables for the evening's hotpot. Mum got the ingredients ready for the steamed fish.
5 天tiān黑hēi了le，餐cān桌zhuō上shàng已yǐ摆bǎi满mǎn了le美měi食shí。我wǒ们men三sān个gè人rén围wéi坐zuò在zài餐cān桌zhuō旁páng，我wǒ一yì边biān给gěi爸bà爸bà妈mā妈mā夹jiā菜cài，一yì边biān说shuō着zhe学xué校xiào的de新xīn鲜xiān事shì。这zhè时shí，爸bà爸bà和hé妈mā妈mā的de手shǒu机jī铃líng声shēng同tóng时shí响xiǎng了le起qǐ来lái，那nà是shì姐jiě姐jiě和hé哥gē哥gē打dǎ来lái的de电diàn话huà。在zài电diàn话huà的de另lìng一yì头tóu，姐jiě姐jiě举jǔ起qǐ杯bēi子zi和hé我wǒ们men“干gān杯bēi”，哥gē哥gē祝zhù我wǒ们men新xīn年nián快kuài乐lè！
5By nightfall, the table was piled with delicious food. The three of us sat down together, and I picked food into my parents' bowls while telling them what was new at school. Just then, Mum's and Dad's phones rang at the same time — it was my sister and my brother. On the other end, my sister raised her glass for a "cheers" with us, and my brother wished us a Happy New Year!
6 爸bà爸bà和hé妈mā妈mā笑xiào得dé合hé不bù上shàng嘴zuǐ，我wǒ们men一yī家jiā人rén终zhōng于yú“团tuán圆yuán”了le！
6Dad and Mum couldn't stop smiling. Our family was finally "reunited"!
