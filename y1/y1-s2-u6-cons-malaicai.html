《80岁卖菜阿婆 从未想过退休》 An 80-Year-Old Vegetable Seller Who Has Never Once Thought of Retiring

语速 Speed 0.9× (set before play)
字体 Size

生词 Vocabulary · 3 words

退tuì休xiū
to retire; retirement
巴bā刹shā
wet market; pasar (Singapore/Malay term)
一yí辈bèi子zi
a lifetime; one's whole life
1 在zài红hóng山shān巴bā刹shā的de一yí个gè摊tān位wèi上shàng，整zhěng整zhěng齐qí齐qí地dì摆bǎi满mǎn了le新xīn鲜xiān的de蔬shū菜cài。摊tān主zhǔ是shì位wèi80岁suì的de老lǎo妇fù，名míng叫jiào林lín亚yà莲lián。她tā从cóng年nián轻qīng一yì直zhí工gōng作zuò到dào今jīn天tiān。
1At a stall in Redhill Market, fresh vegetables are laid out in neat rows. The stallholder is an 80-year-old woman named Lim Ah Lian. She has been working from her younger days right up till today.
2 林lín亚yà莲lián以yǐ前qián一yì直zhí在zài甘gān榜bǎng生shēng活huó，以yǐ种zhòng菜cài、卖mài菜cài为wéi生shēng。甘gān榜bǎng被bèi拆chāi除chú后hòu，她tā一yī家jiā人rén搬bān到dào红hóng山shān的de四sì房fáng式shì组zǔ屋wū居jū住zhù。从cóng那nà时shí开kāi始shǐ，林lín亚yà莲lián就jiù在zài红hóng山shān巴bā刹shā卖mài菜cài。
2Lim Ah Lian used to live in a kampong, making her living by growing and selling vegetables. After the kampong was demolished, her family moved to a four-room HDB flat in Redhill. From then on, she has been selling vegetables at Redhill Market.
3 当dāng年nián，林lín亚yà莲lián以yǐ50多duō元yuán租zū下xià一yí个gè摊tān位wèi。因yīn为wèi子zǐ女nǚ较jiào多duō，一yī家jiā人rén的de生shēng活huó并bìng不bù容róng易yì。回huí忆yì起qǐ当dāng年nián，她tā骄jiāo傲ào地dì说shuō：“那nà时shí虽suī然rán穷qióng，但dàn我wǒ们men凭píng着zhe自zì己jǐ的de双shuāng手shǒu养yǎng活huó了le一yī家jiā人rén，从cóng不bù觉jué得de苦kǔ。”
3Back in the day, Lim Ah Lian rented a stall for just over fifty dollars. With many children, life was not easy. Looking back, she proudly says: "Yes, we were poor — but we fed our family with our own two hands. We never thought of it as suffering."
4 现xiàn在zài，林lín亚yà莲lián年nián纪jì大dà了le，却què一yì点diǎn儿ér也yě不bù糊hú涂tú，算suàn钱qián、找zhǎo钱qián都dōu不bú会huì出chū差chā错cuò。她tā从cóng未wèi想xiǎng过guò退tuì休xiū。她tā说shuō：“我wǒ工gōng作zuò了le一yí辈bèi子zi，只zhǐ要yào还hái有yǒu一yì口kǒu气qì，就jiù会huì继jì续xù工gōng作zuò下xià去qù。”
4Now, even though she's older, Lim Ah Lian's mind is still as sharp as ever — never a mistake when adding up money or giving change. She has never once thought of retiring. She says: "I've worked my whole life. As long as I'm still drawing breath, I'll keep on working."
5 看kàn着zhe满mǎn头tóu白bái发fà的de林lín亚yà莲lián在zài摊tān位wèi上shàng忙máng碌lù的de样yàng子zi，想xiǎng到dào她tā对duì家jiā庭tíng和hé社shè会huì做zuò出chū的de贡gòng献xiàn，我wǒ对duì她tā产chǎn生shēng了le深shēn深shēn的de敬jìng意yì。
5Watching white-haired Lim Ah Lian busying herself at her stall, and thinking of all she has contributed to her family and to society, a deep respect rose up in me.
