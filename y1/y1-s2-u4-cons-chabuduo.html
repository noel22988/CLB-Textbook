《差不多先生》 Mr. Close-Enough

语速 Speed 0.9× (set before play)
字体 Size

why must (one)…?; there's no need to

once; previously (an action that has happened before)

1 有yǒu这zhè么me一yí个gè人rén，他tā姓xìng差chà，名míng不bù多duō。
1There was once a man — his surname was Cha, his given name Buduo. (Together: "Mr. Close-Enough.")
2 他tā常cháng常cháng说shuō：“凡fán事shì只zhǐ要yào差chà不bu多duō就jiù好hǎo了le，何hé必bì太tài认rèn真zhēn呢ne？”
2He often said, "As long as something is close enough, that's good enough. Why be so serious about it?"
3 小xiǎo时shí候hòu，妈mā妈mā叫jiào他tā买mǎi红hóng糖táng，他tā买mǎi了le白bái糖táng。妈mā妈mā骂mà他tā，他tā摇yáo摇yáo头tóu说shuō：“红hóng糖táng白bái糖táng不bú是shì差chà不bu多duō吗ma？”
3When he was little, his mother sent him to buy brown sugar — he bought white sugar instead. When she scolded him, he just shook his head and said, "Isn't brown sugar close enough to white sugar?"
4 他tā曾céng经jīng在zài一yī家jiā店diàn里lǐ做zuò事shì，常cháng常cháng把bǎ“十shí”写xiě成chéng“千qiān”，把bǎ“千qiān”写xiě成chéng“十shí”，害hài得dé店diàn里lǐ亏kuī了le不bù少shǎo钱qián。老lǎo板bǎn气qì得dé骂mà他tā，他tā却què笑xiào嘻xī嘻xī地dì说shuō：“‘千qiān’字zì和hé‘十shí’字zì不bú是shì差chà不bu多duō吗ma？”
4He once worked at a shop, where he kept writing 十 ("ten") as 千 ("thousand"), and 千 as 十 — costing the shop a lot of money. The owner was furious and yelled at him, but he just grinned and said, "Isn't 千 close enough to 十?"
5 有yǒu一yì天tiān，差chà不bu多duō先xiān生shēng得dé了le急jí病bìng，赶gǎn紧jǐn叫jiào家jiā人rén去qù请qǐng东dōng街jiē的de汪wāng大dà夫fū。家jiā人rén一yì时shí找zhǎo不bù着zhe汪wāng大dà夫fū，就jiù把bǎ西xī街jiē的de兽shòu医yī王wáng大dà夫fū请qǐng来lái了le。差chà不bu多duō先xiān生shēng知zhī道dào找zhǎo错cuò了le人rén。但dàn是shì，他tā想xiǎng：王wáng大dà夫fū和hé汪wāng大dà夫fū也yě差chà不bu多duō，让ràng他tā试shì试shì看kàn吧ba。这zhè位wèi王wáng大dà夫fū用yòng医yī牛niú的de方fāng法fǎ给gěi差chà不bu多duō先xiān生shēng治zhì病bìng。结jié果guǒ不bú到dào一yí个gè小xiǎo时shí，差chà不bu多duō先xiān生shēng就jiù断duàn气qì了le。
5One day, Mr. Close-Enough fell suddenly ill, and quickly told his family to fetch Doctor Wang (汪) from East Street. The family couldn't find that Doctor Wang, so they brought Vet Wang (王) from West Street instead. Mr. Close-Enough realised they had got the wrong person — but, he thought, this Wang and that Wang are pretty close — let him have a go. This Vet Wang treated him using the methods he used on cows. Within an hour, Mr. Close-Enough had stopped breathing.
6 差chà不bu多duō先xiān生shēng临lín死sǐ前qián说shuō：“活huó人rén同tóng死sǐ人rén也yě差chà……差chà不bu多duō。凡fán事shì只zhǐ要yào差chà……差chà不bu多duō就jiù好hǎo了le，何hé……何hé必bì太tài……太tài认rèn真zhēn呢ne？”
6On his deathbed, Mr. Close-Enough said: "The living and the dead are also close… close enough. As long as something is close… close enough, that's good enough. Why… why be so… so serious?"
