1 今jīn天tiān，老lǎo师shī带dài我wǒ们men参cān观guān科kē学xué创chuàng新xīn博bó物wù馆guǎn。我wǒ们men从cóng入rù口kǒu进jìn入rù展zhǎn厅tīng。
1Today, our teacher took us to visit the Science and Innovation Museum. We entered the exhibition hall through the entrance.
2 在zài第dì一yī展zhǎn厅tīng里lǐ，我wǒ们men看kàn到dào了le人rén类lèi历lì史shǐ上shàng重zhòng大dà的de发fā现xiàn。跟gēn着zhe地dì上shàng的de箭jiàn头tóu走zǒu，我wǒ们men来lái到dào第dì二èr展zhǎn厅tīng。这zhè里lǐ介jiè绍shào了le世shì界jiè上shàng伟wěi大dà的de发fā明míng和hé发fā明míng家jiā。每měi一yī件jiàn展zhǎn品pǐn都dōu非fēi常cháng精jīng彩cǎi。
2In the first exhibition hall, we saw the major discoveries of human history. Following the arrows on the floor, we came to the second exhibition hall. This one introduced the world's great inventions and inventors. Every exhibit was fascinating.
3 沿yán着zhe走zǒu道dào走zǒu到dào第dì三sān展zhǎn厅tīng，我wǒ们men看kàn到dào了le“我wǒ是shì创chuàng新xīn王wáng”得dé奖jiǎng作zuò品pǐn展zhǎn。学xué生shēng们men用yòng创chuàng意yì解jiě决jué问wèn题tí，设shè计jì产chǎn品pǐn，让ràng人rén佩pèi服fú。最zuì后hòu，我wǒ们men从cóng出chū口kǒu离lí开kāi了le博bó物wù馆guǎn。
3Walking along the walkway to the third exhibition hall, we saw the “I Am an Innovation Champ” award-winners' exhibit. Students used creativity to solve problems and design products — really impressive. Finally, we left the museum through the exit.
