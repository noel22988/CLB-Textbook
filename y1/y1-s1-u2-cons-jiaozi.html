《幸运饺子》 The Lucky Dumpling

语速 Speed 0.9× (set before play)
字体 Size

生词 Vocabulary · 7 words

饺jiǎo子zi
dumplings
幸xìng运yùn
lucky; fortune (幸运饺子 = the lucky dumpling)
大dà吉jí大dà利lì
great fortune and luck (a CNY blessing)
盯dīng
to stare at; to fix one's eyes on
疼téng爱ài
to dote on; to love dearly (esp. of a younger family member)
趁chèn
while (the moment is right); to take advantage of (an opportunity)
浓nóng
thick; deep (of feeling, flavour, fragrance)
1 每měi年nián的de大dà年nián三sān十shí，我wǒ们men全quán家jiā都dū会huì围wéi坐zuò在zài一yì起qǐ包bāo饺jiǎo子zi。
1Every Lunar New Year's Eve, our whole family sits down together to wrap dumplings.
2 “我wǒ把bǎ红hóng枣zǎo放fàng进jìn去qù，谁shuí吃chī到dào这zhè个ge幸xìng运yùn饺jiǎo子zi，今jīn年nián就jiù会huì心xīn想xiǎng事shì成chéng，大dà吉jí大dà利lì！”姑gū姑gū说shuō。
2"I'm putting a red date inside," said Auntie. "Whoever eats this lucky dumpling will have everything go their way this year — every blessing imaginable!"
3 我wǒ紧jǐn盯dīng着zhe幸xìng运yùn饺jiǎo子zi。饺jiǎo子zi煮zhǔ好hǎo一yī上shàng桌zhuō，我wǒ就jiù马mǎ上shàng把bǎ幸xìng运yùn饺jiǎo子zi夹jiā给gěi奶nǎi奶nǎi，因yīn为wèi奶nǎi奶nǎi最zuì疼téng爱ài我wǒ。奶nǎi奶nǎi对duì我wǒ笑xiào了le笑xiào，然rán后hòu把bǎ幸xìng运yùn饺jiǎo子zi夹jiā到dào妈mā妈mā的de碗wǎn里lǐ。
3I kept my eyes glued to the lucky dumpling. The moment the dumplings were cooked and brought to the table, I picked up the lucky one with my chopsticks and put it into Grandma's bowl, because Grandma loves me the most. Grandma smiled at me — and then picked up the lucky dumpling and put it into Mum's bowl.
4 妈mā妈mā趁chèn大dà家jiā不bù注zhù意yì，又yòu和hé姑gū姑gū换huàn了le碗wǎn。
4While no one was looking, Mum swapped her bowl with Auntie's.
5 突tū然rán，我wǒ大dà叫jiào一yī声shēng：“我wǒ吃chī到dào幸xìng运yùn饺jiǎo子zi了le！”姑gū姑gū拍pāi拍pāi我wǒ的de肩jiān膀bǎng，亲qīn切qiè地dì说shuō：“你nǐ年nián纪jì最zuì小xiǎo，是shì我wǒ们men家jiā最zuì大dà的de希xī望wàng，祝zhù你nǐ学xué业yè进jìn步bù，健jiàn康kāng成chéng长zhǎng！”
5All of a sudden, I shouted, "I got the lucky dumpling!" Auntie patted my shoulder and said warmly, "You're the youngest — you're our family's greatest hope. May you do well in your studies and grow up healthy and strong!"
6 我wǒ笑xiào了le，一yī家jiā人rén都dōu笑xiào了le。饺jiǎo子zi里lǐ那nà份fèn浓nóng浓nóng的de亲qīn情qíng，让ràng我wǒ们men心xīn里lǐ暖nuǎn暖nuǎn的de。
6I smiled, and the whole family smiled with me. The deep family love inside that dumpling left a warm glow in all our hearts.
