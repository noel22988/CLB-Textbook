Eight Good Habits
Make a Plan. Think through what you'll do before you do it, and follow your plan to the end.
Be Punctual, Value Time. Be on time for appointments, and treasure every minute.
Live Healthily. Sleep early, wake early, eat plenty of vegetables and fruit.
Keep Your Word. If you promised, you do it.
See It Through. When you hit a difficulty, find a way to overcome it.
Be Honest. Be truthful as a person — don't lie.
Treat Others with Courtesy. When interacting with others, be polite.
Read Often. Read regularly, and care about what's going on in the world.

Quick Survey: Do You Have These Good Habits?
How to Take the Survey. Pair up with a classmate. For each habit, mark down: Often / Sometimes / Never. When you're done, share your findings with each other.
Survey Items. 1. Sleep early, wake early.
2. Eat vegetables and fruit.
3. Treasure time and finish homework on schedule.
4. When you run into a problem, find ways to overcome it.
5. Read the news and care about world events.
6. Be punctual when meeting friends.
7. Be very honest — never lie.
8. Don't peek during tests.
9. Take the initiative to greet others.
