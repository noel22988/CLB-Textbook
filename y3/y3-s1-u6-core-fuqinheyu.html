1 阿ā丽lì到dào机jī场chǎng去qù接jiē远yuǎn道dào而ér来lái的de父fù亲qīn。自zì从cóng阿ā丽lì和hé丈zhàng夫fū结jié婚hūn搬bān到dào斗dòu湖hú后hòu，她tā和hé父fù亲qīn相xiāng聚jù的de时shí间jiān就jiù变biàn少shǎo了le。她tā的de父fù亲qīn每měi几jǐ年nián都dū会huì去qù那nà里lǐ探tàn望wàng她tā。
1Ah Li went to the airport to pick up her father, who had come from far away. Ever since marrying her husband and moving to Tawau, time with her father had become scarce. Every few years, he would make the trip to visit her.
2 阿ā丽lì在zài机jī场chǎng接jiē到dào父fù亲qīn，心xīn里lǐ很hěn高gāo兴xìng，但dàn她tā不bù习xí惯guàn表biǎo现xiàn得dé太tài亲qīn热rè，只zhǐ轻qīng轻qīng地de叫jiào了le一yī声shēng：“爸bà爸bà。” 父fù亲qīn看kàn到dào女nǚ儿ér阿ā丽lì，脸liǎn上shàng的de表biǎo情qíng显xiǎn得de十shí分fēn严yán肃sù，其qí实shí他tā心xīn里lǐ也yě是shì高gāo兴xìng的de。
2Meeting her father at the airport, Ah Li was happy in her heart — but she wasn't used to showing too much affection, and only called out softly, "Pa." Seeing his daughter Ah Li, her father's face stayed perfectly serious — even though inside, he too was glad.
3 晚wǎn餐cān时shí间jiān到dào了le，父fù亲qīn看kàn到dào餐cān桌zhuō上shàng的de菜cài，表biǎo情qíng有yǒu些xiē僵jiāng硬yìng，一yí句jù话huà也yě不bù说shuō。
3Dinnertime came. Seeing the food on the table, Father's face went a little stiff, and he didn't say a word.
4 看kàn到dào父fù亲qīn似sì乎hū有yǒu些xiē不bù开kāi心xīn，阿ā丽lì便biàn把bǎ桌zhuō上shàng那nà盘pán煎jiān得dé香xiāng喷pēn喷pēn的de小xiǎo鱼yú推tuī到dào他tā面miàn前qián：“爸bà爸bà，这zhè是shì您nín最zuì爱ài吃chī的de。”
4Sensing her father seemed unhappy, Ah Li pushed the plate of fragrant pan-fried little fish in front of him: "Pa, these are your favourite."
5 “我wǒ最zuì爱ài吃chī的de？”父fù亲qīn惊jīng讶yà地dì看kàn着zhe阿ā丽lì，“谁shuí告gào诉sù你nǐ的de？”
5"My favourite?" Father looked at Ah Li in astonishment. "Who told you that?"
6 是shì啊a，谁shuí说shuō的de呢ne？这zhè种zhǒng小xiǎo鱼yú虽suī然rán可kě口kǒu，但dàn是shì华huá人rén认rèn为wéi那nà是shì贱jiàn价jià的de海hǎi鲜xiān。
6Yes — who had said so? These little fish were tasty, but Chinese people considered them cheap seafood.
7 阿ā丽lì记jì得de很hěn清qīng楚chǔ，小xiǎo时shí候hòu，家jiā里lǐ小xiǎo孩hái都dōu抢qiǎng着zhe吃chī这zhè种zhǒng小xiǎo鱼yú。自zì己jǐ时shí常cháng因yīn为wèi抢qiǎng不bú到dào鱼yú而ér哭kū。尽jǐn管guǎn父fù亲qīn嘴zuǐ上shàng骂mà她tā爱ài哭kū，却què还hái是shì把bǎ自zì己jǐ碗wǎn里lǐ的de鱼yú夹jiā给gěi她tā。长zhǎng大dà后hòu，每měi次cì父fù亲qīn要yào来lái，阿ā丽lì就jiù会huì去qù买mǎi这zhè种zhǒng她tā心xīn目mù中zhōng最zuì美měi味wèi的de鱼yú。
7Ah Li remembered clearly: when she was little, the kids at home would all scramble to eat these little fish. She often cried when she didn't get any. Father would grumble that she was a crybaby — but then he'd take the fish from his own bowl and put it in hers. After she grew up, whenever her father was coming to visit, Ah Li would go and buy these fish — the most delicious fish in her heart.
8 “我wǒ以yǐ为wéi……爸bà爸bà爱ài吃chī。”阿ā丽lì吞tūn吞tūn吐tǔ吐tǔ地dì说shuō，“我wǒ们men家jiā以yǐ前qián常cháng吃chī的de，爸bà爸bà忘wàng记jì了le吗ma？我wǒ……我wǒ从cóng小xiǎo就jiù很hěn爱ài吃chī。”
8"I thought… Pa loved them," Ah Li said haltingly. "We used to eat them often at home — has Pa forgotten? I… I've loved them since I was little."
9 父fù亲qīn一yí下xià子zi明míng白bái了le：这zhè小xiǎo鱼yú竟jìng然rán是shì女nǚ儿ér心xīn中zhōng最zuì美měi味wèi的de海hǎi鲜xiān。以yǐ前qián，每měi当dāng他tā要yào去qù探tàn望wàng女nǚ儿ér，朋péng友yǒu们men都dōu很hěn羡xiàn慕mù。他tā们men总zǒng是shì说shuō：“斗dòu湖hú的de海hǎi鲜xiān是shì全quán马mǎ来lái西xī亚yà最zuì便pián宜yi，最zuì好hǎo吃chī的de，你nǐ的de女nǚ儿ér一yí定dìng用yòng大dà鱼yú大dà虾xiā招zhāo待dài你nǐ。”可kě是shì饭fàn桌zhuō上shàng却què总zǒng是shì这zhè种zhǒng小xiǎo鱼yú，他tā每měi次cì看kàn到dào，心xīn中zhōng都dōu很hěn不bú是shì滋zī味wèi。
9Father understood all at once: these little fish were the most delicious seafood in his daughter's heart. In the past, every time he was about to go visit her, his friends were full of envy. They'd always say, "Tawau has the cheapest, tastiest seafood in all of Malaysia — your daughter must be feasting you on big fish and prawns." But on her dinner table it was always these little fish — and every time he saw them, something stirred uneasily in his heart.
10 原yuán来lái女nǚ儿ér是shì爱ài自zì己jǐ的de！但dàn父fù女nǚ都dōu把bǎ爱ài意yì藏cáng在zài心xīn里lǐ，没méi有yǒu说shuō出chū口kǒu。父fù亲qīn夹jiā起qǐ一yī条tiáo小xiǎo鱼yú，小xiǎo口kǒu小xiǎo口kǒu地dì吃chī着zhe，好hǎo像xiàng在zài细xì细xì品pǐn味wèi女nǚ儿ér的de一yī番fān心xīn意yì。
10All this time — his daughter had loved him! But both father and daughter had kept their love tucked away in their hearts, never spoken aloud. Father picked up a little fish, eating it bite by bite — as if slowly savouring his daughter's heartfelt gesture.
