1 学xué校xiào每měi年nián都dōu举jǔ行xíng体tǐ能néng测cè验yàn，我wǒ总zǒng是shì不bù及jí格gé，尤yóu其qí是shì长cháng跑pǎo，我wǒ常cháng常cháng最zuì后hòu一yí个gè抵dǐ达dá终zhōng点diǎn。今jīn年nián，我wǒ告gào诉sù自zì己jǐ：不bù能néng再zài跑pǎo最zuì后hòu一yī名míng了le。
1Every year our school holds a fitness test, and I always fail — especially the long-distance run, where I'm usually the last to reach the finish line. This year, I told myself: no more coming in last.
2 为wèi了le顺shùn利lì通tōng过guò体tǐ能néng测cè验yàn，我wǒ做zuò了le很hěn多duō准zhǔn备bèi。我wǒ每měi天tiān练liàn习xí跑pǎo步bù，三sān餐cān都dōu吃chī得dé很hěn健jiàn康kāng，晚wǎn上shàng也yě准zhǔn时shí睡shuì觉jiào，希xī望wàng自zì己jǐ的de体tǐ力lì可kě以yǐ变biàn得dé更gèng好hǎo。
2To pass the test, I prepared in many ways. I ran every day, ate healthy meals three times a day, and slept on time at night — hoping my fitness would improve.
3 一yī开kāi始shǐ，我wǒ跑pǎo十shí分fēn钟zhōng就jiù累lèi了le。过guò了le一yí段duàn时shí间jiān，我wǒ发fā现xiàn自zì己jǐ能néng够gòu坚jiān持chí的de时shí间jiān越yuè来lái越yuè长zhǎng，而ér且qiě也yě不bù觉jué得dé累lèi。不bù只zhǐ是shì这zhè样yàng，我wǒ还hái发fā现xiàn自zì己jǐ的de精jīng神shén变biàn得dé比bǐ以yǐ前qián更gèng好hǎo，上shàng课kè也yě更gèng专zhuān心xīn了le。
3At first, I'd get tired after just ten minutes of running. After a while, I realised I could keep going longer and longer, and I didn't feel as tired. Not only that — I also felt more alert, and concentrated better in class.
4 我wǒ期qī待dài已yǐ久jiǔ的de体tǐ能néng测cè验yàn终zhōng于yú到dào了le。哨shào子zi一yī响xiǎng，那nà些xiē擅shàn长cháng跑pǎo步bù的de同tóng学xué飞fēi快kuài地dì向xiàng前qián冲chōng，我wǒ在zài后hòu面miàn努nǔ力lì地dì追zhuī赶gǎn。我wǒ心xīn想xiǎng：我wǒ练liàn习xí了le那nà么me久jiǔ，今jīn年nián不bù能néng再zài拿ná最zuì后hòu一yī名míng了le。跑pǎo了le一yí会huì儿r，我wǒ开kāi始shǐ感gǎn到dào呼hū吸xī急jí促cù，脚jiǎo步bù沉chén重zhòng，但dàn还hái是shì用yòng尽jǐn所suǒ有yǒu的de力lì气qì跑pǎo到dào终zhōng点diǎn。
4The fitness test I'd been waiting for finally came. As soon as the whistle blew, the classmates who were good at running shot forward, and I worked hard to keep up behind them. I thought: I've trained so long — I can't come last again this year. After running a while, I started gasping for breath, my steps felt heavy — but I used every ounce of strength to make it to the finish line.
5 到dào达dá终zhōng点diǎn时shí，我wǒ回huí头tóu一yī看kàn，发fā现xiàn自zì己jǐ还hái是shì最zuì后hòu一yī名míng！
5When I crossed the line, I turned around — and saw I was still last!
6 我wǒ正zhèng难nán过guò着zhe，体tǐ育yù老lǎo师shī走zǒu了le过guò来lái，笑xiào着zhe对duì我wǒ说shuō：“太tài棒bàng了le！你nǐ战zhàn胜shèng了le自zì己jǐ，这zhè次cì及jí格gé了le！继jì续xù努nǔ力lì！”听tīng了le老lǎo师shī的de话huà，我wǒ心xīn里lǐ一yí下xià子zi轻qīng松sōng了le起qǐ来lái。
6I was feeling down when the PE teacher came over and said to me with a smile, "Well done! You beat your own time — you passed this time! Keep at it!" Hearing his words, my heart suddenly felt lighter.
7 体tǐ能néng测cè验yàn之zhī后hòu，我wǒ仍réng然rán坚jiān持chí每měi天tiān跑pǎo步bù。因yīn为wèi运yùn动dòng是shì提tí升shēng自zì己jǐ最zuì好hǎo的de方fāng式shì，让ràng我wǒ们men的de生shēng活huó更gèng健jiàn康kāng。
7After the fitness test, I kept up my daily running. Because exercise is the best way to better yourself — and it makes life healthier for all of us.
