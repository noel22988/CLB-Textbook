1 小xiǎo芳fāng因yīn为wèi眼yǎn睛jīng看kàn不bú见jiàn，每měi天tiān都dōu戴dài着zhuó眼yǎn罩zhào。一yì天tiān，医yī生shēng对duì她tā说shuō：“我wǒ们men找zhǎo到dào了le眼yǎn角jiǎo膜mó的de捐juān赠zèng者zhě。”
1Xiaofang couldn't see, and wore an eye patch every day. One day, the doctor said to her: “We've found a cornea donor.”
2 在zài等děng待dài手shǒu术shù的de日rì子zi里lǐ，姐jiě姐jiě带dài她tā到dào海hǎi边biān，让ràng她tā用yòng手shǒu触chù摸mō贝bèi壳ké。
2During the days of waiting for the surgery, her elder sister took her to the seaside and let her feel the shells with her hands.
3 姐jiě姐jiě告gào诉sù她tā：“贝bèi壳ké经jīng过guò海hǎi浪làng的de冲chōng洗xǐ，残cán缺quē不bù全quán。摸mō的de时shí候hòu，才cái会huì有yǒu层céng次cì感gǎn。”小xiǎo芳fāng轻qīng轻qīng地de摸mō了le摸mō，说shuō：“贝bèi壳ké的de边biān缘yuán凹āo凹āo凸tū凸tū的de，摸mō起qǐ来lái像xiàng音yīn符fú一yí样yàng。”原yuán来lái，世shì界jiè的de美měi丽lì不bù只zhǐ是shì用yòng眼yǎn睛jīng看kàn，还hái可kě以yǐ用yòng心xīn去qù感gǎn受shòu。
3Her sister told her, “The shells, washed by the waves, are chipped and broken. Only when you touch them do you feel the layers.” Xiaofang touched them gently and said, “The edges of the shells are rough and uneven — they feel like musical notes.” It turned out, the beauty of the world isn't only for the eyes — you can also feel it with your heart.
