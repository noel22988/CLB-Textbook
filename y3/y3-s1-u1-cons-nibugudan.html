2We saw Yongming shove Dayou hard, and snarl: "I'm the only one willing to be your friend. You'd better hand it over — or else…" Yongming raised his fist as he spoke. Dayou shrank into a frightened ball.
3 “陈chén老lǎo师shī快kuài来lái！这zhè里lǐ有yǒu人rén打dǎ架jià！”伟wěi华huá大dà喊hǎn。永yǒng明míng一yī听tīng到dào“陈chén老lǎo师shī”三sān个gè字zì，就jiù马mǎ上shàng溜liū走zǒu了le。我wǒ赶gǎn紧jǐn跑pǎo过guò去qù把bǎ大dà友yǒu扶fú起qǐ来lái。大dà友yǒu一yī脸liǎn苍cāng白bái，身shēn体tǐ仍réng在zài发fā抖dǒu。
3"Mr Chen, come quick! Someone's fighting!" Weihua shouted. The moment Yongming heard "Mr Chen," he made himself scarce. I rushed over to help Dayou up. His face was pale, and he was still shaking.
4 “你nǐ和hé永yǒng明míng不bú是shì好hǎo朋péng友yǒu吗ma？他tā怎zěn么me会huì欺qī负fù你nǐ？”伟wěi华huá问wèn。
4"Aren't you and Yongming good friends?" Weihua asked. "Why would he bully you?"
5 “他tā才cái不bú是shì我wǒ的de朋péng友yǒu，他tā发fā现xiàn我wǒ是shì网wǎng络luò游yóu戏xì的de高gāo手shǒu，就jiù利lì用yòng我wǒ，还hái逼bī我wǒ把bǎ游yóu戏xì里lǐ收shōu集jí到dào的de装zhuāng备bèi给gěi他tā。我wǒ不bù肯kěn，他tā就jiù……”
5"He's not my friend at all. When he found out I was an expert at online games, he started using me — even forcing me to hand over the gear I'd collected in-game. When I refused, he…"
6 我wǒ听tīng了le，很hěn为wèi大dà友yǒu打dǎ抱bào不bù平píng，心xīn想xiǎng一yí定dìng要yào帮bāng助zhù他tā。为wèi了le不bù让ràng大dà友yǒu沉chén迷mí网wǎng络luò游yóu戏xì，我wǒ便biàn对duì他tā说shuō：“大dà友yǒu，假jià期qī快kuài到dào了le，我wǒ和hé伟wěi华huá做zuò了le一yí个gè‘假jià期qī活huó动dòng清qīng单dān’，每měi天tiān一yì起qǐ去qù运yùn动dòng、学xué习xí。你nǐ要yào不bù要yào加jiā入rù？”
6Listening to this, I felt furious on Dayou's behalf and decided I had to help him. To stop Dayou getting sucked into online games, I said, "Dayou — the holidays are coming up. Weihua and I have put together a 'holiday activity list' — exercising and studying together every day. Want to join in?"
