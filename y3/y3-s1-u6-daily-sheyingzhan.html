Wu Jingming, Sec 2
About the Photo. On my way to school, I saw a car stopped in the middle of the road, unable to move — the driver looked panic-stricken. Just then, a lorry full of migrant workers pulled up. The workers all jumped down and helped push the car to the side of the road. What they did really moved me.
