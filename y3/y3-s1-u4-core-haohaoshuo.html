1 最zuì近jìn，老lǎo师shī把bǎ我wǒ、玉yù文wén、甜tián甜tián和hé班bān长zhǎng美měi华huá组zǔ成chéng一yī队duì，参cān加jiā学xué校xiào的de接jiē力lì赛sài跑pǎo。我wǒ心xīn里lǐ想xiǎng：玉yù文wén反fǎn应yìng那nà么me慢màn，老lǎo师shī怎zěn么me会huì选xuǎn她tā代dài表biǎo我wǒ们men班bān参cān加jiā体tǐ育yù竞jìng赛sài呢ne？
1Recently, our teacher put me, Yuwen, Tiantian, and the class monitor Meihua together as a team for the school relay race. I thought to myself: Yuwen reacts so slowly — why would the teacher pick her to represent our class in a sports event?
2 练liàn习xí时shí，每měi当dāng我wǒ把bǎ接jiē力lì棒bàng递dì给gěi玉yù文wén，她tā都dū会huì愣lèng几jǐ秒miǎo才cái接jiē过guò去qù。我wǒ们men练liàn习xí了le好hǎo几jǐ次cì，玉yù文wén仍réng然rán没méi有yǒu什shén么me进jìn步bù。
2During practice, every time I handed her the baton, Yuwen would freeze for a few seconds before taking it. We practised several times, and she still wasn't getting any better.
3 有yǒu一yī次cì，我wǒ忍rěn不bú住zhù朝cháo她tā大dà喊hǎn：“玉yù文wén，怎zěn么me那nà么me慢màn！动dòng作zuò快kuài点diǎn儿ér好hǎo吗ma？”大dà概gài是shì我wǒ说shuō话huà的de语yǔ气qì太tài差chà，她tā看kàn起qǐ来lái有yǒu些xiē委wěi屈qu，小xiǎo声shēng说shuō道dào：“行xíng啦la，下xià次cì我wǒ快kuài点diǎn儿ér就jiù是shì！”我wǒ们men又yòu练liàn了le几jǐ次cì，她tā还hái是shì老lǎo样yàng子zi。我wǒ气qì得dé把bǎ接jiē力lì棒bàng摔shuāi在zài地dì上shàng，大dà声shēng责zé备bèi她tā：“你nǐ还hái是shì这zhè么me慢màn，一yì点diǎn儿ér也yě没méi进jìn步bù！”玉yù文wén没méi有yǒu说shuō什shén么me，但dàn眼yǎn眶kuàng已yǐ经jīng有yǒu些xiē湿shī润rùn，似sì乎hū马mǎ上shàng就jiù要yào哭kū出chū来lái。
3Once, I couldn't help shouting at her: "Yuwen, why are you so slow! Can you speed up?" My tone must have been pretty harsh — she looked hurt, and said quietly, "Fine, I'll be faster next time!" We practised a few more times, and she was still the same. Fed up, I threw the baton on the ground and scolded her loudly: "You're still this slow — not an ounce of improvement!" Yuwen didn't say anything, but her eyes were already misting up, as if she might burst into tears at any moment.
4 班bān长zhǎng赶gǎn忙máng跑pǎo过guò来lái。她tā面miàn带dài微wēi笑xiào，温wēn和hé而ér耐nài心xīn地dì对duì玉yù文wén说shuō：“你nǐ接jiē棒bàng时shí要yào集jí中zhōng注zhù意yì力lì，看kàn到dào队duì友yǒu跑pǎo过guò来lái时shí就jiù要yào准zhǔn备bèi接jiē棒bàng。”她tā一yì边biān说shuō，一yì边biān给gěi玉yù文wén演yǎn示shì接jiē棒bàng的de动dòng作zuò。玉yù文wén静jìng静jìng地de听tīng着zhe，看kàn着zhe，然rán后hòu点diǎn点diǎn头tóu表biǎo示shì明míng白bái。班bān长zhǎng拍pāi拍pāi玉yù文wén的de肩jiān膀bǎng，鼓gǔ励lì她tā继jì续xù练liàn习xí。我wǒ站zhàn在zài一yī旁páng看kàn着zhe，心xīn想xiǎng：我wǒ是shì不bú是shì应yīng该gāi像xiàng班bān长zhǎng那nà样yàng有yǒu话huà好hǎo好hǎo说shuō？
4Our class monitor hurried over. With a smile on her face, she said gently and patiently to Yuwen: "When you take the baton, you have to concentrate — as soon as you see your teammate running over, get ready to receive it." As she spoke, she demonstrated the handover for Yuwen. Yuwen listened quietly, watched, then nodded to show she understood. Meihua patted Yuwen's shoulder and encouraged her to keep practising. I stood there watching, and thought: Shouldn't I be talking to people kindly, the way the class monitor does?
5 比bǐ赛sài当dàng天tiān，我wǒ们men几jǐ个gè接jiē力lì队duì员yuán配pèi合hé得dé很hěn好hǎo，默mò契qì十shí足zú，还hái得dé了le第dì二èr名míng！比bǐ赛sài结jié束shù后hòu，我wǒ高gāo兴xìng地dì跑pǎo上shàng前qián拥yōng抱bào玉yù文wén，轻qīng声shēng对duì她tā说shuō：“对duì不bù起qǐ，我wǒ应yīng该gāi好hǎo好hǎo跟gēn你nǐ说shuō话huà。发fā脾pí气qì是shì不bù能néng解jiě决jué问wèn题tí的de。”玉yù文wén笑xiào着zhe说shuō：“没méi事shì，你nǐ也yě是shì为wèi了le团tuán队duì着zhuó想xiǎng。”
5On race day, our relay team worked together really well, full of rapport — and we came in second! After the race, I happily ran over to hug Yuwen and said quietly, "I'm sorry — I should have spoken to you properly. Losing my temper wasn't going to solve anything." Yuwen smiled and said, "It's okay — you were thinking of the team too."
6 经jīng过guò这zhè件jiàn事shì，我wǒ领lǐng悟wù了le一yí个gè道dào理lǐ：有yǒu话huà应yīng该gāi好hǎo好hǎo说shuō。有yǒu了le良liáng好hǎo的de沟gōu通tōng，问wèn题tí才cái能néng迎yíng刃rèn而ér解jiě。
6Through all this, I came to understand something: if you've got something to say, say it kindly. With good communication, problems can be resolved easily.
