1 为wèi了le丰fēng富fù我wǒ们men的de学xué习xí体tǐ验yàn，老lǎo师shī鼓gǔ励lì我wǒ们men在zài课kè余yú时shí间jiān，去qù学xué习xí自zì己jǐ感gǎn兴xìng趣qù的de东dōng西xī。我wǒ平píng时shí喜xǐ欢huān喝hē珍zhēn珠zhū奶nǎi茶chá，刚gāng好hǎo可kě以yǐ借jiè这zhè个ge机jī会huì，学xué习xí如rú何hé制zhì作zuò珍zhēn珠zhū奶nǎi茶chá。
1To enrich our learning experience, our teacher encouraged us to use our spare time to learn something we're interested in. I love drinking bubble tea, so this was the perfect chance to learn how to make it myself.
2 我wǒ上shàng网wǎng找zhǎo资zī料liào，发fā现xiàn一yī杯bēi珍zhēn珠zhū奶nǎi茶chá的de含hán糖táng量liàng竟jìng然rán高gāo达dá20茶chá匙chí。糖táng太tài多duō，不bù利lì于yú健jiàn康kāng。于yú是shì，我wǒ决jué定dìng尝cháng试shì制zhì作zuò一yī杯bēi低dī糖táng的de珍zhēn珠zhū奶nǎi茶chá。
2Looking up information online, I was shocked to find that one cup of bubble tea can contain up to 20 teaspoons of sugar. Way too much sugar — not good for health. So I decided to try making a low-sugar version myself.
3 我wǒ准zhǔn备bèi了le材cái料liào，一yì边biān看kàn视shì频pín，一yì边biān跟gēn着zhe步bù骤zhòu做zuò。
3I prepared the ingredients, watching a video and following each step.
材cái料liào
Ingredients
4 木mù薯shǔ粉fěn80克kè；少shǎo量liàng黑hēi糖táng；少shǎo量liàng冰bīng糖táng；红hóng茶chá包bāo3个gè；牛niú奶nǎi500毫háo升shēng；开kāi水shuǐ40毫háo升shēng。
480 g tapioca starch; a small amount of brown sugar; a small amount of rock sugar; 3 black tea bags; 500 ml milk; 40 ml boiling water.
珍zhēn珠zhū奶nǎi茶chá的de制zhì作zuò步bù骤zhòu
How to Make Bubble Tea
5 首shǒu先xiān，煮zhǔ奶nǎi茶chá。将jiāng500毫háo升shēng的de牛niú奶nǎi、3个gè红hóng茶chá包bāo和hé少shǎo量liàng的de冰bīng糖táng放fàng入rù锅guō中zhōng，用yòng小xiǎo火huǒ煮zhǔ开kāi，直zhí到dào冰bīng糖táng溶róng化huà，牛niú奶nǎi变biàn成chéng浅qiǎn棕zōng色sè。
5First, brew the milk tea. Put 500 ml of milk, 3 tea bags, and a small amount of rock sugar into a pot. Bring it to a gentle simmer over low heat — until the rock sugar dissolves and the milk turns light brown.
6 其qí次cì，准zhǔn备bèi用yòng来lái制zhì作zuò“珍zhēn珠zhū”的de面miàn团tuán。将jiāng少shǎo量liàng的de黑hēi糖táng和hé40毫háo升shēng的de开kāi水shuǐ倒dào入rù锅guō中zhōng，快kuài速sù搅jiǎo拌bàn让ràng黑hēi糖táng溶róng化huà。然rán后hòu，加jiā入rù80克kè的de木mù薯shǔ粉fěn，快kuài速sù搅jiǎo拌bàn成chéng面miàn团tuán。
6Next, prepare the dough for the "pearls." Pour a small amount of brown sugar and 40 ml of boiling water into a pot, and stir quickly to dissolve the sugar. Then add 80 g of tapioca starch and stir rapidly to form a dough.
7 接jiē着zhe，制zhì作zuò“珍zhēn珠zhū”。先xiān将jiāng面miàn团tuán切qiè成chéng几jǐ份fèn，把bǎ每měi一yī份fèn都dōu拉lā成chéng细xì长cháng条tiáo，再zài切qiè成chéng小xiǎo块kuài，一yī一yī揉róu搓cuō成chéng“珍zhēn珠zhū”。将jiāng水shuǐ烧shāo开kāi，放fàng入rù“珍zhēn珠zhū”，煮zhǔ15分fēn钟zhōng。将jiāng“珍zhēn珠zhū”取qǔ出chū后hòu，放fàng入rù冰bīng水shuǐ中zhōng，这zhè样yàng会huì更gèng有yǒu弹tán性xìng。
7Now, make the pearls. Cut the dough into a few portions; pull each into long thin strips, then cut into small pieces. Roll each piece into a pearl with your hands. Bring water to a boil, drop in the pearls, and cook for 15 minutes. Take the pearls out and put them in ice water — this makes them springier.
8 最zuì后hòu，倒dào入rù奶nǎi茶chá。在zài杯bēi中zhōng放fàng入rù“珍zhēn珠zhū”，然rán后hòu将jiāng奶nǎi茶chá倒dào入rù，搅jiǎo拌bàn几jǐ下xià，就jiù完wán成chéng了le！
8Finally, pour in the milk tea. Place the pearls in a cup, pour the milk tea over them, stir a few times — and you're done!
9 原yuán来lái，制zhì作zuò健jiàn康kāng低dī糖táng的de珍zhēn珠zhū奶nǎi茶chá一yì点diǎn儿ér都dōu不bù难nán。喝hē着zhe亲qīn手shǒu制zhì作zuò的de饮yǐn料liào，我wǒ心xīn中zhōng充chōng满mǎn了le幸xìng福fú感gǎn和hé成chéng就jiù感gǎn。
9It turns out making a healthy, low-sugar bubble tea isn't difficult at all. Sipping the drink I'd made with my own hands, my heart filled with happiness — and a real sense of achievement.
