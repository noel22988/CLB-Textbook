Ms Li · Opening the Forum
Topic. How to Build Good Relationships With Others. Everyone is welcome to share thoughts in the forum and discuss.
话huà题tí一yī：以yǐ诚chéng相xiāng待dài
小xiǎo跳tiào我wǒ们men应yīng该gāi真zhēn诚chéng地dì对duì待dài他tā人rén，表biǎo达dá自zì己jǐ的de真zhēn实shí想xiǎng法fǎ。
甜tián甜tián表biǎo达dá真zhēn实shí的de想xiǎng法fǎ时shí要yào注zhù意yì方fāng法fǎ，不bù能néng太tài直zhí接jiē，更gèng不bù能néng显xiǎn得de粗鲁cū，免lǔ得，伤miǎn感de情shāng。
Topic 1: Be Sincere With Others
Xiaotiao. We should treat others sincerely and express our genuine thoughts.
Tiantian. When you express your real thoughts, mind how you do it — don't be too blunt, and definitely don't come across as rude. You don't want to hurt the relationship.
话huà题tí二èr：懂dǒng得de退tuì让ràng
义yì阳yáng当dāng我wǒ们men和hé别bié人rén的de意yì见jiàn不bù同tóng时shí，要yào好hǎo好hǎo沟gōu通tōng，必bì要yào时shí要yào懂dǒng得de妥协tuǒ与xié退让yǔ。
世shì亮liàng大dà家jiā各gè让ràng一yī步bù，才cái能néng解jiě决jué问wèn题tí。
Topic 2: Know When to Give Way
Yiyang. When we disagree with others, we should communicate properly — and when needed, we should know how to compromise and give way.
Shiliang. Each side gives a little — that's how problems get solved.
话huà题tí三sān：相xiāng互hù理lǐ解jiě
小xiǎo双shuāng每měi个gè人rén的de性格xìng和gé成长hé背chéng景zhǎng都bèi不jǐng同dōu。我bù们tóng要wǒ懂men得yào站dǒng在de别zhàn人zài的bié角rén度de去jiǎo看dù问qù题kàn，为wèn对tí方wèi着duì想fāng，这zhuó样xiǎng人zhè与yàng人rén之yǔ间rén的zhī关jiān系de才guān会xì更cái和huì谐gèng。
云yún飞fēi要yào做zuò到dào相xiāng互hù理lǐ解jiě，就jiù要yào认rèn真zhēn听tīng对duì方fāng说shuō话huà，明míng白bái他tā的de想xiǎng法fǎ。
Topic 3: Understand Each Other
Xiaoshuang. Everyone's personality and background are different. We need to learn to look at things from the other person's point of view and consider what they're going through — that's what makes relationships more harmonious.
Yunfei. To really understand each other, you have to listen carefully to what the other person is saying and grasp where they're coming from.
话huà题tí四sì：尊zūn重zhòng对duì方fāng
国guó强qiáng我wǒ们men要yào尊zūn重zhòng朋péng友yǒu的de想xiǎng法fǎ和hé感gǎn受shòu，也yě要yào尊zūn重zhòng他tā们men的de隐私yǐn。
丽lì华huá是shì的de。如rú果guǒ我wǒ们men随suí便biàn把bǎ朋péng友yǒu的de隐私yǐn告sī诉别gào人sù，会bié令rén朋huì友lìng感péng到yǒu不gǎn愉dào快bù。
Topic 4: Respect Each Other
Guoqiang. We should respect our friends' thoughts and feelings — and also respect their privacy.
Lihua. Yes. If we casually share a friend's private business with others, it'll make our friend unhappy.
