1 我wǒ的de同tóng学xué小xiǎo敏mǐn患huàn了le癌ái症zhèng，需xū要yào长cháng期qī治zhì疗liáo。她tā每měi星xīng期qī都dōu要yào去qù医yī院yuàn做zuò化huà疗liáo，因yīn此cǐ常cháng常cháng缺quē课kè。
1My classmate Xiaomin has cancer and needs long-term treatment. Every week she has to go to the hospital for chemotherapy, so she often misses classes.
2 我wǒ和hé几jǐ位wèi同tóng学xué每měi个gè周zhōu末mò都dōu去qù她tā家jiā分fēn享xiǎng上shàng课kè的de笔bǐ记jì，陪péi她tā一yì起qǐ温wēn习xí功gōng课kè。一yī开kāi始shǐ大dà家jiā都dōu觉jué得de不bù自zì在zài，但dàn渐jiàn渐jiàn地de，我wǒ们men和hé小xiǎo敏mǐn成chéng了le好hǎo朋péng友yǒu。
2A few classmates and I go to her home every weekend to share class notes and revise schoolwork with her. At first everyone felt a little awkward, but gradually we became good friends with Xiaomin.
3 上shàng个gè月yuè，学xué校xiào为wèi儿ér童tóng癌ái症zhèng基jī金jīn会huì筹chóu款kuǎn。为wèi了le支zhī持chí小xiǎo敏mǐn，我wǒ和hé几jǐ位wèi同tóng学xué一yì起qǐ剃tì掉diào头tóu发fā，希xī望wàng让ràng她tā知zhī道dào，她tā并bìng不bù孤gū单dān。
3Last month, the school raised funds for the children's cancer foundation. To support Xiaomin, a few classmates and I shaved off our hair together — hoping to show her she's not alone.
