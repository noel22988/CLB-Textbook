1 周zhōu末mò，妈mā妈mā带dài小xiǎo华huá去qù商shāng场chǎng买mǎi新xīn球qiú鞋xié。两liǎng人rén看kàn了le很hěn久jiǔ，挑tiāo选xuǎn了le几jǐ双shuāng。妈mā妈mā指zhǐ着zhe其qí中zhōng一yī双shuāng说shuō：“这zhè双shuāng球qiú鞋xié也yě不bù错cuò，还hái打dǎ了le折zhé，价jià格gé合hé理lǐ，就jiù买mǎi这zhè双shuāng吧ba。”
1On the weekend, Mum takes Xiaohua to the shopping mall to buy new sneakers. After looking for a long time, they pick out a few pairs. Mum points to one and says, “These sneakers are good too — and they're on discount, the price is reasonable. Let's get these.”
2 小xiǎo华huá一yī脸liǎn不bù高gāo兴xìng，开kāi始shǐ大dà声shēng抱bào怨yuàn道dào：“这zhè不bú是shì我wǒ要yào的de牌pái子zi！新xīn鞋xié又yòu怎zěn样yàng？又yòu不bú是shì名míng牌pái，我wǒ才cái不bú要yào！我wǒ同tóng学xué都dōu穿chuān名míng牌pái的de，我wǒ也yě想xiǎng要yào！”
2Xiaohua's face falls, and he starts to complain loudly: “This isn't the brand I want! What if they're new? They're not a brand name — I don't want them! All my classmates wear brand names, I want one too!”
3 妈mā妈mā听tīng了le，叹tàn了le一yì口kǒu气qì，认rèn真zhēn地dì说shuō道dào：“孩hái子zi，这zhè是shì你nǐ妈mā妈mā辛xīn苦kǔ赚zhuàn钱qián买mǎi给gěi你nǐ的de。我wǒ们men要yào懂dǒng得de珍zhēn惜xī自zì己jǐ所suǒ拥yōng有yǒu的de，人rén生shēng才cái会huì更gèng幸xìng福fú。”
3Mum hears this, lets out a sigh, and says earnestly: “Child — this was bought for you with money your mum worked hard to earn. Only when you learn to treasure what you have will life be happier.”
