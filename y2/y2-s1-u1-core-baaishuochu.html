1 女nǚ儿ér常cháng常cháng称chēng赞zàn我wǒ。每měi次cì我wǒ写xiě完wán文wén章zhāng念niàn给gěi她tā听tīng，她tā总zǒng是shì带dài着zhe崇chóng拜bài的de眼yǎn神shén看kàn着zhe我wǒ。我wǒ读dú完wán后hòu，她tā会huì说shuō：“写xiě得dé真zhēn好hǎo！”每měi当dāng我wǒ穿chuān上shàng新xīn衣yī服fú，女nǚ儿ér总zǒng会huì夸kuā赞zàn：“好hǎo漂piào亮liàng！”看kàn到dào女nǚ儿ér双shuāng眼yǎn含hán笑xiào，一yī脸liǎn欣xīn赏shǎng的de样yàng子zi，我wǒ的de心xīn情qíng也yě变biàn得dé无wú比bǐ愉yú快kuài。
1My daughter is always praising me. Every time I finish writing an article and read it to her, she watches me with admiring eyes. When I'm done, she says: "That was so good!" Whenever I put on a new outfit, she'll exclaim, "So pretty!" Seeing her smiling eyes and that look of genuine admiration, my mood lifts no end.
2 儿ér子zi却què不bù一yí样yàng。他tā经jīng常cháng泼pō冷lěng水shuǐ：“妈mā，您nín别bié相xiāng信xìn妹mèi妹mèi的de甜tián言yán蜜mì语yǔ，她tā是shì在zài拍pāi马mǎ屁pi！”
2My son is different. He's forever throwing cold water on it: "Mum, don't fall for your sister's sweet talk — she's just buttering you up!"
3 我wǒ的de抽chōu屉tì里lǐ有yǒu很hěn多duō女nǚ儿ér写xiě给gěi我wǒ的de卡kǎ片piàn。每měi次cì看kàn到dào这zhè些xiē卡kǎ片piàn，我wǒ就jiù觉jué得de再zài辛xīn苦kǔ也yě是shì值zhí得de的de。
3I have a drawer full of cards my daughter has made for me. Every time I see them, I feel that no matter how hard things get, it's all worth it.
4 那nà年nián，父fù亲qīn过guò世shì，母mǔ亲qīn一yí下xià子zi老lǎo了le很hěn多duō。她tā变biàn得dé沉chén默mò寡guǎ言yán。于yú是shì，我wǒ接jiē她tā来lái和hé我wǒ们men同tóng住zhù。
4The year my father passed away, my mother seemed to age all at once. She became quiet and reserved. So I brought her to live with us.
5 母mǔ亲qīn每měi天tiān帮bāng我wǒ煮zhǔ饭fàn做zuò菜cài，打dǎ理lǐ家jiā务wù。我wǒ很hěn感gǎn激jī母mǔ亲qīn，心xīn想xiǎng：我wǒ应yīng该gāi学xué学xué女nǚ儿ér，勇yǒng敢gǎn地dì向xiàng母mǔ亲qīn表biǎo达dá感gǎn谢xiè，表biǎo达dá爱ài。可kě是shì，一yī看kàn到dào母mǔ亲qīn，我wǒ却què说shuō不bù出chū口kǒu。
5Every day, Mum helped with the cooking and the housework. I was so grateful to her, and thought: I should learn from my daughter — I should work up the courage to thank her, to tell her I love her. But every time I looked at her, the words wouldn't come.
6 一yì天tiān吃chī午wǔ餐cān时shí，我wǒ想xiǎng：不bù能néng再zài错cuò过guò机jī会huì了le。我wǒ鼓gǔ起qǐ勇yǒng气qì说shuō：“妈mā，我wǒ……我wǒ觉jué得de自zì己jǐ好hǎo幸xìng福fú！谢xiè谢xiè您nín！我wǒ……爱ài您nín！”
6One day at lunch, I thought: I can't let another chance slip by. Gathering my courage, I said: "Mum, I… I feel so lucky. Thank you. I… I love you."
7 说shuō完wán，我wǒ拿ná起qǐ包bāo冲chōng出chū家jiā门mén，不bù敢gǎn看kàn母mǔ亲qīn的de表biǎo情qíng。
7The moment I'd said it, I grabbed my bag and rushed out the door, not daring to look at her face.
8 那nà天tiān下xià班bān回huí家jiā，我wǒ听tīng见jiàn母mǔ亲qīn在zài唱chàng歌gē。自zì从cóng父fù亲qīn过guò世shì后hòu，我wǒ还hái是shì第dì一yī次cì听tīng到dào这zhè熟shú悉xī的de歌gē声shēng。
8That evening, when I came home from work, I heard Mum singing. It was the first time I'd heard that familiar voice in song since Dad had passed.
