《最美的姿势》 The Most Beautiful Pose

语速 Speed 0.9× (set before play)
字体 Size

生词 Vocabulary · 4 words

脖bó子zi
neck
姿zī势shì
pose; posture
展zhǎn现xiàn
to show; to display (one's best side)
思sī考kǎo
to think (deeply); reflection
1 小xiǎo学xué时shí，一yī场cháng车chē祸huò导dǎo致zhì我wǒ的de脖bó子zi歪wāi向xiàng右yòu边biān。从cóng那nà以yǐ后hòu，我wǒ害hài怕pà别bié人rén的de眼yǎn光guāng，也yě讨tǎo厌yàn拍pāi照zhào。
1In primary school, a car accident left my neck tilted permanently to the right. From then on, I dreaded people's looks — and I hated being photographed.
2 一yì天tiān，喜xǐ欢huān拍pāi照zhào的de陈chén老lǎo师shī提tí议yì办bàn一yí个gè照zhào片piān展zhǎn。她tā会huì为wèi每měi个gè同tóng学xué拍pāi一yī张zhāng个gè人rén照zhào，然rán后hòu贴tiē在zài布bù告gào板bǎn上shàng，展zhǎn现xiàn大dà家jiā最zuì美měi的de一yí面miàn。同tóng学xué们men都dōu很hěn兴xīng奋fèn。
2One day, Ms Chen — a photography enthusiast — proposed an exhibition. She would take an individual portrait of every student and put them up on the noticeboard, showing each person's most beautiful side. Everyone was excited.
3 “宜yí欣xīn，快kuài来lái拍pāi照zhào！”陈chén老lǎo师shī说shuō。我wǒ慢màn吞tūn吞tūn地dì走zǒu上shàng前qián，心xīn里lǐ一yí片piàn慌huāng乱luàn，感gǎn觉jué同tóng学xué们men都dōu注zhù视shì着zhe我wǒ。
3"Yixin, come on — your turn!" said Ms Chen. Slowly I walked forward, my heart in a mess, feeling every classmate's eyes on me.
4 “宜yí欣xīn，你nǐ觉jué得de你nǐ最zuì美měi的de姿zī势shì是shì什shén么me？”陈chén老lǎo师shī问wèn。我wǒ摇yáo摇yáo头tóu，低dī声shēng说shuō：“我wǒ没méi有yǒu。”
4"Yixin, what do you think is your most beautiful pose?" asked Ms Chen. I shook my head and said quietly, "I don't have one."
5 陈chén老lǎo师shī笑xiào着zhe说shuō：“你nǐ错cuò了le，这zhè个ge世shì界jiè上shàng每měi个gè人rén都dōu有yǒu自zì己jǐ最zuì美měi丽lì的de姿zī势shì！”这zhè时shí，热rè心xīn的de甜tián甜tián跑pǎo向xiàng我wǒ，教jiào我wǒ摆bǎi出chū像xiàng是shì在zài思sī考kǎo的de样yàng子zi。“咔kā嚓chā”一yī声shēng，这zhè个ge姿zī势shì定dìng格gé在zài陈chén老lǎo师shī的de相xiàng机jī里lǐ。
5Ms Chen smiled and said, "You're wrong! Everyone in this world has their own most beautiful pose!" Just then, warm-hearted Tiantian came over to me and showed me how to pose as if deep in thought. "Click!" — the pose was captured in Ms Chen's camera.
6 第dì二èr天tiān早zǎo上shàng，甜tián甜tián把bǎ我wǒ拉lā到dào布bù告gào板bǎn前qián，指zhǐ着zhe我wǒ的de照zhào片piān说shuō：“你nǐ看kàn你nǐ的de照zhào片piān多duō好hǎo看kàn！”其qí他tā同tóng学xué纷fēn纷fēn点diǎn头tóu，大dà家jiā你nǐ一yī言yán我wǒ一yī语yǔ，温wēn暖nuǎn的de话huà语yǔ令lìng我wǒ非fēi常cháng感gǎn动dòng。
6The next morning, Tiantian pulled me over to the noticeboard, pointed at my photo and said, "Look how lovely your picture is!" The other classmates nodded in agreement, joining in with their own warm words. I was deeply moved.
7 从cóng那nà以yǐ后hòu，我wǒ变biàn得dé开kāi朗lǎng又yòu自zì信xìn，还hái结jié交jiāo了le许xǔ多duō朋péng友yǒu。
7From that day on, I became cheerful and confident, and made many friends.
8 原yuán来lái，我wǒ也yě可kě以yǐ拥yōng有yǒu最zuì美měi的de姿zī势shì，我wǒ也yě可kě以yǐ很hěn美měi！每měi个gè人rén都dōu有yǒu不bù完wán美měi的de地dì方fāng，但dàn是shì我wǒ们men都dōu可kě以yǐ找zhǎo到dào自zì己jǐ最zuì美měi的de姿zī势shì，都dōu可kě以yǐ活huó得dé自zì信xìn，活huó出chū精jīng彩cǎi。
8It turned out — I too could have my most beautiful pose. I too could be beautiful! Everyone has their imperfections — but every one of us can find our own most beautiful pose, every one of us can live with confidence and live a life that shines.
