我wǒ国guó的de水shuǐ从cóng哪nǎ里lǐ来lái？
Where Does Singapore's Water Come From?
1 新xīn加jiā坡pō的de水shuǐ供gōng来lái源yuán：新xīn生shēng水shuǐ、淡dàn化huà水shuǐ、收shōu集jí的de雨yǔ水shuǐ、进jìn口kǒu水shuǐ。我wǒ国guó的de四sì种zhǒng水shuǐ资zī源yuán叫jiào做zuò“四sì大dà水shuǐ喉hóu”。
1Singapore's water comes from four sources: NEWater, desalinated water, collected rainwater, and imported water. Together, they are known as Singapore's "Four National Taps."
家jiā里lǐ的de水shuǐ都dōu用yòng在zài哪nǎ里lǐ了le？
Where Does Household Water Go?
2 洗xǐ澡zǎo 29%；冲chōng马mǎ桶tǒng 17%；处chǔ理lǐ食shí物wù 16%；洗xǐ衣yī 15%；洗xǐ手shǒu洗xǐ脸liǎn 12%；清qīng洗xǐ餐cān具jù 7%；其qí他tā 4%。
2Showering 29%; flushing toilets 17%; food preparation 16%; laundry 15%; washing hands and face 12%; washing dishes 7%; other 4%.
3 家jiā庭tíng用yòng水shuǐ最zuì多duō的de地dì方fāng是shì浴yù室shì，其qí中zhōng洗xǐ澡zǎo用yòng水shuǐ就jiù超chāo过guò每měi人rén每měi日rì平píng均jūn用yòng水shuǐ量liàng的de四sì分fēn之zhī一yī。
3The bathroom uses the most household water — showers alone account for more than a quarter of each person's daily average water use.
我wǒ国guó每měi人rén每měi日rì平均píng用jūn水量yòng及shuǐ2030年liàng目jí标nián
Average Daily Use Per Person, and the 2030 Target
4 我wǒ国guó的de目mù标biāo是shì：2030年nián每měi人rén每měi日rì平píng均jūn使shǐ用yòng130公gōng升shēng的de水shuǐ。
4Singapore's goal: 130 litres per person per day, by 2030.
5 水shuǐ是shì宝bǎo贵guì的de，每měi滴dī水shuǐ都dōu来lái得de不bù容róng易yì。我wǒ们men要yào节jié省shěng用yòng水shuǐ，珍zhēn惜xī水shuǐ资zī源yuán。
5Water is precious. Not a drop comes easily. We should conserve water — and treasure this resource.
