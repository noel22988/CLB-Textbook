1 我wǒ的de室shì友yǒu蒋jiǎng妍yán有yǒu晚wǎn睡shuì早zǎo起qǐ的de习xí惯guàn。她tā每měi晚wǎn都dōu开kāi着zhe灯dēng，苦kǔ读dú到dào十shí二èr点diǎn，使shǐ我wǒ和hé其qí他tā室shì友yǒu不bù能néng好hǎo好hǎo休xiū息xī。更gèng让ràng人rén讨tǎo厌yàn的de是shì，我wǒ们men每měi天tiān清qīng晨chén都dōu被bèi她tā的de闹nào钟zhōng吵chǎo醒xǐng！我wǒ和hé她tā说shuō了le几jǐ次cì，她tā却què不bù理lǐ。
1My roommate Jiang Yan has the habit of staying up late and waking up early. Every night she keeps the light on and burns the midnight oil studying until twelve, leaving the rest of us unable to get proper rest. Even worse, every morning we get blasted awake by her alarm clock! I've said something to her a few times — she just ignores me.
2 一yì天tiān早zǎo晨chén，我wǒ又yòu被bèi她tā的de闹nào钟zhōng吵chǎo醒xǐng。我wǒ气qì得dé朝cháo她tā大dà喊hǎn。从cóng此cǐ，我wǒ们men彻chè底dǐ成chéng了le“冤yuān家jiā”。
2One morning, jolted awake by her alarm again, I lost my temper and shouted at her. After that, we became completely at odds with each other.
3 一yí个gè周zhōu六liù晚wǎn上shàng，宿sù舍shè里lǐ只zhǐ剩shèng下xià我wǒ们men两liǎng个gè人rén，我wǒ们men谁shuí也yě不bù理lǐ谁shuí。
3One Saturday night, only the two of us were left in the dorm, and neither of us would speak to the other.
4 忽hū然rán，门mén外wài传zhuàn来lái转zhuǎn动dòng门mén把bà手shǒu的de声shēng音yīn。我wǒ和hé蒋jiǎng妍yán同tóng时shí朝cháo门mén的de方fāng向xiàng望wàng去qù。门mén慢màn慢màn开kāi了le一yī条tiáo缝fèng，似sì乎hū有yǒu一yí个gè人rén影yǐng站zhàn在zài门mén外wài，看kàn不bù清qīng样yàng子zi，像xiàng是shì一yí个gè长cháng发fà女nǚ生shēng。
4Suddenly, there was the sound of a door handle turning outside. Jiang Yan and I both whipped our heads round to the door at the same moment. The door slowly opened just a crack — a figure seemed to be standing on the other side, but we couldn't make out the face clearly. It looked like a long-haired girl.
5 我wǒ拉lā开kāi门mén，伸shēn出chū头tóu看kàn了le看kàn，走zǒu廊láng上shàng什shén么me也yě没méi有yǒu。我wǒ赶gǎn紧jǐn锁suǒ上shàng门mén，关guān灯dēng上shàng床chuáng。
5I pulled the door open and poked my head out. The corridor was empty — there was nothing there. I quickly locked the door, turned off the light, and got into bed.
6 黑hēi暗àn中zhōng，蒋jiǎng妍yán问wèn了le一yí句jù：“你nǐ看kàn见jiàn那nà个ge女nǚ鬼guǐ了le吗ma？”“看kàn见jiàn了le。”我wǒ的de声shēng音yīn有yǒu些xiē抖dǒu。那nà一yí夜yè，我wǒ们men聊liáo了le很hěn多duō。我wǒ们men发fā现xiàn我wǒ俩liǎ有yǒu很hěn多duō共gòng同tóng的de爱ài好hào。蒋jiǎng妍yán也yě为wèi之zhī前qián的de事shì情qíng跟gēn我wǒ道dào了le歉qiàn。
6In the dark, Jiang Yan asked, "Did you see that ghost girl?" "I saw her," I said, my voice slightly shaky. That night, the two of us talked for hours. We discovered we had a lot of interests in common, and Jiang Yan even apologised for everything before.
7 从cóng那nà天tiān起qǐ，我wǒ们men成chéng了liǎo无wú所suǒ不bù谈tán的de朋péng友yǒu。
7From that day on, we became friends who could talk to each other about anything.
8 几jǐ天tiān后hòu，我wǒ在zài宿sù舍shè楼lóu下xià遇yù到dào那nà个ge“女nǚ鬼guǐ”。原yuán来lái她tā是shì新xīn来lái的de同tóng学xué，就jiù住zhù在zài楼lóu上shàng，那nà天tiān走zǒu错cuò了le宿sù舍shè。多duō么me可kě怕pà而ér美měi丽lì的de误wù会huì啊a！
8A few days later, I bumped into the "ghost girl" downstairs. It turned out she was a new student living on the floor above us — that night she'd just come to the wrong dorm. What a terrifying — and beautiful — misunderstanding it had been!
9 因yīn为wèi这zhè起qǐ“恐kǒng怖bù事shì件jiàn”，我wǒ和hé蒋jiǎng妍yán变biàn成chéng了le好hǎo朋péng友yǒu。其qí实shí，很hěn多duō时shí候hòu，只zhǐ要yào多duō点diǎn交jiāo流liú，人rén与yǔ人rén之zhī间jiān的de关guān系xì就jiù会huì变biàn得dé更gèng和hé谐xié。
9Because of that "horror incident," Jiang Yan and I became close friends. The truth is, many times, just a little more communication is all it takes to make our relationships much more harmonious.
